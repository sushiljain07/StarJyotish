## Chapter 3: Amatyakaraka

**Amatyakaraka** (AMK) is from Jaimini astrology. It is the planet with the **second-highest degree** in the birth chart (after the Atmakaraka which has the highest degree).

### Key Definitions

- **Atmakaraka** = Highest degree planet = "King" of the horoscope (the soul's purpose)
- **Amatyakaraka** = Second-highest degree planet = "Minister" of the horoscope
- The Amatyakaraka is the planet through which the Atmakaraka tries to fulfill its wish — making it a **primary career significator**
- **Association (conjunction or mutual aspect) of Atmakaraka and Amatyakaraka = Raj Yoga of First Order**

### How to Calculate Amatyakaraka

1. Note the degrees of all 7 planets: Sun, Moon, Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn
2. Rank them from highest to lowest degree (ignore Rahu/Ketu)
3. The planet with the **highest degree** = Atmakaraka
4. The planet with the **2nd highest degree** = Amatyakaraka

### Different Planets as Amatyakaraka (Official Table)

| AMK Planet | Career Attributes |
|------------|------------------|
| **Sun** | Leadership roles as Government servant, Administrative officer, Magistrate, Doctor, Politician |
| **Moon** | Mental work, Philosopher, Spiritual Person, Business in Liquids, Silver, etc. |
| **Mercury** | Business, Education, Teachers, Auditors, Marketing |
| **Venus** | Creative jobs, Acting, Singing, Graphics designing, Fashion designing |
| **Mars** | Armed forces, Military, Security services, Surgeon, Investigator, Athletics, etc. |
| **Saturn** | Government servant, Social worker, Mass leader, Judicial officer or Judge |
| **Jupiter** | Teachers, Professors, Financial advisor, Scholars, Priests, Religious leaders |

## Chapter 4: Basics of D10 Chart (Dashamsha)

The **D10 (Dashamsha)** is a divisional chart that divides each rashi into 10 equal parts (3° each). It is the most important divisional chart for career analysis.

> **Read `references/d10-analysis.md`** for the complete D10 framework including: house attributes in D10, key planet indicators (Sun/Saturn/Moon), the full 10th lord placement table (Houses 1-12), Ruling Deities of Dashamsha, and the Service vs. Business determination method.

### What D10 Analyzes

D10 reveals: type of career and job stability, professional perception, work ethic and responsibilities, periods of advancement or challenges, and authority/leadership potential.

### D10 House Meanings (Summary)

| House | D10-Specific Career Meaning |
|-------|---------------------------|
| 1st | Career beginning, perception, karma strength |
| 2nd | Resources and speech for career |
| 3rd | Creativity and communication in career |
| 4th | Comfort/happiness from profession |
| 5th | Professional status, career well-wishers |
| 6th | Rivalries and career obstacles |
| 7th | Business associates and partners |
| 8th | Hidden career problems, end of career |
| 9th | Rightful path of profession, mentors |
| **10th** | **Status achieved, immediate boss** |
| 11th | Gains from career |
| 12th | End of career, foreign/secluded work |

### Key Indicators in D10

- **D10 Lagna lord** — Overall career approach and public life
- **10th lord of D1 placed in D10** — Where it falls gives specific career results
- **Sun in angle (Kendra) of D10** → Government job with high status; Sun in 6th/8th/12th → problems from government
- **Saturn strongly placed in D10** → Support from subordinates, political career; Weakly placed → conflicts with subordinates
- **Moon in D10** → 10th lord from Moon = mental attitude to work; Strong Moon = work enthusiasm; Weak Moon = inferiority complex
- **Raj Yoga in D10** → High position and status in profession
- **Dhana Yoga in D10** → Wealth from multiple professions

### Planet Placement Strength in D10

Planets in **Kendra (angles)** are most powerful. Order of strength: **10th > 7th > 4th > 1st**. Even malefics in kendras give career support.

Planets in **Trine (5th, 9th)** are auspicious if unafflicted. If afflicted, they bring career changes and obstacles (since 5th and 9th are the 8th and 12th from the 10th house).

### Key Principle

> A planet strong in both D1 and D10 gives strong career results. A planet weak in D1 but strong in D10 can still deliver career results. A planet strong in D1 but weak in D10 may not fully deliver.

### Service vs. Business: 6th House vs. 7th House

This is a critical determination:
- **6th house stronger** → Service/employment orientation
- **7th house stronger** → Business/self-employment orientation
- **Both equally strong** → Native does both (job + business)

## Chapter 5: Analysis of Profession — Step-by-Step Method

### Step 1: Identify Key Significators

- Lagna lord (personality and drive)
- 10th house lord in D1 (primary career indicator)
- 10th house lord in D10
- Planets in 10th house (D1 and D10)
- Amatyakaraka planet
- 6th lord (for job/service) or 7th lord (for business)

### Step 2: Check for Job vs. Business (4-Method Framework)

Use all four methods together for a definitive answer:

**Method 1 — House Strength:** Is 6th lord stronger → Job; 7th lord stronger → Business

**Method 2 — House Connections:** 6th lord linked to 2nd/11th → Job income; 7th lord linked to 2nd/11th → Business income

**Method 3 — Ashtakvarga Bindus:** Higher bindus in 6th → Job; higher in 7th → Business

**Method 4 — D10 Confirmation:** Compare 6th vs. 7th house strength in D10 as well

> See `references/career-combinations.md` for the full framework and 12 specific planetary career combinations

### Step 3: Determine Industry/Field

- Identify the dominant planets connected to 10th house (by placement, lordship, aspect)
- Cross-reference with Amatyakaraka
- Check which planet is strongest (own sign, exalted, or receiving benefic aspects)
- Use the Karakas table in `references/karakas.md`

### Step 4: Assess Strength

- Is the 10th lord in good dignity (exalted, own sign, friendly sign)?
- Is the 10th house free from malefic influence?
- Is Amatyakaraka well-placed in D10?

## Chapter 6: Predicting Promotion

### Key Indicators of Promotion

1. **Dasha/Antardasha** of the 10th lord, 11th lord, or Amatyakaraka
2. **Jupiter transiting** over natal 10th lord, 10th house, or Lagna
3. **Saturn completing Sadesati** or moving away after a difficult phase
4. **Sun transit** activating the 10th house in a favorable period
5. Strong **Dasha-Antardasha** combinations involving 1st, 5th, 9th, 10th, 11th house lords

### When to Predict Promotion

- When the running Dasha lord is the 10th or 11th lord
- When Jupiter transits 10th, 11th, or 1st house from Lagna or Moon
- When benefic planets transit the natal 10th lord
- When D10 chart shows strong activation of its 10th or 1st house

## Chapter 7: Predicting Difficult Career Phases & Career Breaks

### Indicators of Difficult Career Phases

1. **Dasha/Antardasha** of 6th, 8th, or 12th house lords
2. **Saturn or Rahu** transiting the 10th house
3. **Sadesati** (Saturn's 7.5-year transit) especially the peak middle phase
4. **Ketu Dasha** — often causes disconnection, loss of interest, career breaks
5. **Afflicted 10th lord** running its Dasha
6. **D10 chart** showing malefic influence on Lagna or 10th house

### Types of Career Breaks

| Cause | Indicator |
|-------|-----------|
| Health-related break | 6th/8th house activation, Sun/Mars affliction |
| Family/personal reasons | 4th/12th house activation, Moon affliction |
| Layoff/job loss | 8th lord Dasha + Saturn transit on 10th |
| Voluntary sabbatical | 12th house activation, Ketu Dasha |
| Travel/foreign opportunity | 12th house benefic activation, Rahu influence |

## Chapter 8: Predicting Timing of Job/Career Change

### Key Timing Triggers

1. **Dasha change** — Each major Dasha shift often brings career change
2. **Saturn transit over natal Moon, Sun, or 10th lord** — Forces restructuring
3. **Rahu/Ketu transit** over the 10th house axis — Sudden changes, foreign opportunities
4. **Jupiter transit** over 10th house — New opportunities, growth
5. **Antardasha of planets** connected to both 6th (current job) and 10th (new career direction)

### Confirming Career Change (Multi-Factor Check)

For a confirmed career change prediction, look for at least 3 of the following:
- Dasha/Antardasha supports change
- Jupiter or Saturn transiting key houses
- D10 Lagna lord or 10th lord activated by transit
- Amatyakaraka under strong influence
- 12th house (endings) activated in D1 or D10

## How to Use This Skill

When a user asks a career astrology question:

1. **Ask for birth details** if not provided: date, time, place of birth
2. **Identify the Lagna** (ascendant), 10th house sign, and key planets
3. **Determine the Amatyakaraka** from the planetary degrees
4. **Read `references/karakas.md`** to match planets to career domains
5. **Assess D10 chart** if available
6. **Check current Dasha/Antardasha** for timing questions
7. **Synthesize** all factors to give a comprehensive answer
8. **Offer remedies** where relevant from Chapter 9

Always combine multiple factors — no single planet or house tells the full story.
