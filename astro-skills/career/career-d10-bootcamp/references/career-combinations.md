## SECTION A: ASHTAKVARGA METHOD — Job vs. Business

**Ashtakvarga** is a predictive tool that assigns points (bindus) to each house, indicating the strength or weakness of planetary influences.

### How to Use Ashtakvarga for Career Orientation

| Ashtakvarga Result | Career Direction |
|-------------------|-----------------|
| **Higher bindus in 6th house** → Go for **JOB** | Indicates strong prospects for jobs, services, and working under others. The individual excels in structured, disciplined environments. |
| **Higher bindus in 7th house** → Go for **BUSINESS** | Suggests success in business, partnerships, or self-employment. Indicates the ability to take risks and lead entrepreneurial ventures. |

## SECTION B: HOUSE CONNECTIONS FOR JOB VS. BUSINESS

### Business Indicator: 7th House + 2nd/11th House Connection

**The combination:** If the 7th house lord is connected with the 2nd house (wealth) or 11th house (gains) through:
- Placement (7th lord placed in 2nd or 11th house)
- Aspect (7th lord aspecting 2nd or 11th house/lord)
- Conjunction (7th lord conjunct 2nd or 11th house lord)

**Why it works:**
- 7th house = partnerships, trade, and external interactions
- 2nd house = finances, accumulated wealth, and family assets
- Their connection shows ability to generate wealth through business dealings, independent ventures, or partnerships

---

### Job Indicator: 6th House + 2nd/11th House Connection

**The combination:** If the 6th house lord is connected with the 2nd house (wealth) or 11th house (income) through:
- Placement (6th lord placed in 2nd or 11th house)
- Aspect (6th lord aspecting 2nd or 11th house/lord)
- Conjunction (6th lord conjunct 2nd or 11th house lord)

**Why it works:**
- 6th house = routine work, discipline, and serving under others
- 2nd house = financial stability; 11th house = gains through structured efforts
- Connection here suggests prosperity through employment, regular income, and promotions within organizational frameworks

## SECTION F: COMPLETE SERVICE vs. BUSINESS DECISION FRAMEWORK

When determining job vs. business, use ALL three methods together:

### Method 1: House Strength (D1 chart)
- Is the 6th house lord stronger (better placed, more dignified) than 7th? → Job
- Is the 7th house lord stronger? → Business
- Are both equally strong? → Both job and business at different life phases

### Method 2: House Connections
- 6th lord connected to 2nd/11th → Financial gains through employment → **Job**
- 7th lord connected to 2nd/11th → Financial gains through trade/business → **Business**

### Method 3: Ashtakvarga Bindus
- Higher bindus in 6th house → **Job**
- Higher bindus in 7th house → **Business**

### Confirming with D10
- Which is stronger in D10: the 6th or 7th house?
- Where does D10 Lagna lord sit — closer to 6th or 7th themes?

### Additional Signals
- **Saturn/Sun dominant, connected to 10th** → Service/government job orientation
- **Mercury/Venus/Jupiter dominant, connected to 10th** → Business/creative/advisory orientation
- **Rahu connected to 10th** → Unconventional business, foreign employment, technology
- **Mahadasha of 7th lord running** → Favors business during that period, even if 6th is otherwise stronger
