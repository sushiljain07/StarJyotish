# D10 Chart (Dashamsha) — Complete Analysis Guide

Source: The Career Compass (D10 Bootcamp)

---

## What D10 (Dasamsha) Analyzes

The D10 chart is prepared specifically to analyze:
- Type of career, job stability, and overall accomplishments
- How a person is perceived in their professional sphere
- Approach to work ethic and responsibilities
- Periods of advancement or challenges in career
- Authority and leadership skills

## Key Indicators in D10 Chart

### Primary Indicators to Check First

1. **Ascendant (Lagna) of D10** — Represents the overall approach to career and public life
2. **10th House Lord of D1 chart in D10** — Where does it fall in D10? (See placement table below)
3. **Placement of Lagna of D10 chart within D10** — Its house position and dignity
4. **Strength of 10th House of D10 and position of its lord** — The apex of career achievement

---

### Placement of Sun in D10

| Sun Placement | Result |
|---------------|--------|
| In angle (Kendra: 1st, 4th, 7th, 10th) | Government jobs with high status and income |
| In 6th, 8th, or 12th house | Problems from government; obstacles in career |

---

### Placement of Saturn in D10

| Saturn Placement | Result |
|-----------------|--------|
| Strongly placed (own sign, exalted, or in angle) | Support from subordinates and workers; career in politics |
| Weakly placed (debilitated, in enemy sign, or afflicted) | Conflicts with subordinates |

### Placement of Moon in D10

| Moon Placement | Result |
|---------------|--------|
| 10th lord from Moon in D10 | Depicts mental attitude towards work/karma |
| Strongly placed Moon | The native has zeal and enthusiasm for work |
| Weakly placed Moon | The native develops inferiority complex |

---

### Yogas in D10

- **Raj Yoga in D10** → High position and status in profession
- **Dhana Yogas in D10** → Wealth from multiple professions

---

### Planets in Angle (Kendra) in D10

Kendra houses: 1st, 4th, 7th, 10th

- Most powerful to deliver good results for career
- Even malefic planets in kendras provide support
- **Order of strength: 10th > 7th > 4th > 1st**

### Planets in Trine in D10

Trine houses: 5th, 9th

- Planets in 5th & 9th house and their lords are auspicious, **if not afflicted**
- Important note: The 5th and 9th houses in D10 are the 8th and 12th respectively from the 10th house
- Therefore, if **afflicted**, they tend to bring **obstacles and changes** to career

Each 3° division of a rashi in D10 has a ruling deity, direction, and planet. This is used for advanced D10 analysis.

## Decoding Career Orientation: Service or Business?

### The Core Question: 6th House vs. 7th House

This is one of the most important determinations in career astrology.

| Factor | Points to Service/Job (6th house) | Points to Business (7th house) |
|--------|-----------------------------------|-------------------------------|
| **Primary house** | 6th house strong, well-placed lord | 7th house strong, well-placed lord |
| **Planets favoring** | Saturn, Sun (in service/admin) | Mercury, Venus, Jupiter (entrepreneurial) |
| **Lagna lord** | Connected to 6th lord | Connected to 7th lord |
| **10th lord connection** | To 6th house/lord | To 7th house/lord |
| **D10 Lagna** | Occupied or aspected by 6th lord | Occupied or aspected by 7th lord |

### Decision Rules

**Service/Job more likely when:**
- 6th house or its lord is stronger than 7th
- Saturn connects to 10th house/lord (discipline, service orientation)
- Sun dominates the 10th (government or corporate job)
- More planets in fixed signs (stability in one place/organization)

**Business more likely when:**
- 7th house or its lord is stronger than 6th
- Mercury or Jupiter strongly placed and connected to 10th
- Rahu in 10th or connected to 10th lord (unconventional, entrepreneurial)
- Venus dominant with 7th house connection (luxury goods, art business)
- More planets in cardinal signs (initiating, independent ventures)

**Both Service AND Business:**
- When both 6th and 7th are equally strong → native may do both (job + side business)
- 11th house very strong → gains from multiple sources
- Dhana yoga in D10 → wealth from multiple professions

### Important Note
Always compare D1 and D10 together. If D1 says job but D10 says business (or vice versa), the native likely transitions from one to the other, with timing shown by Dasha.
