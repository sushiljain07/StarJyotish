| House | Sanskrit Name | Primary Domain | Career Relevance |
|-------|--------------|----------------|-----------------|
| 1st | Lagna | Body, personality, self | How you present yourself professionally; overall life direction |
| 2nd | Dhana | Money, family, speech, food | Accumulated savings; family business; voice-based careers |
| 3rd | Sahaj | Siblings, courage, communication, short trips | Self-effort; media/writing careers; entrepreneurial courage |
| 4th | Sukha | Mother, home, property, vehicles | Real estate; property deals; agriculture; comfort industries |
| 5th | Putra | Children, creativity, education, speculation | Creative industries; speculative markets; teaching; politics |
| 6th | Ripu/Roga | Enemies, disease, debt, **daily job**, servants | **Service/employment**; health professions; legal disputes |
| 7th | Yuvati | Spouse, **business**, partnerships, public | **Business and trade**; partnership-based work; public dealings |
| 8th | Randhra | Death, occult, longevity, inheritance, research | Research; investigation; insurance; occult sciences |
| 9th | Bhagya | Father, luck, higher education, religion, travel | Higher learning; foreign connections; law; religion |
| 10th | Karma | **Career**, karma, authority, social status | **Primary career house**; reputation; public standing |
| 11th | Labha | Gains, income, elder siblings, social circle | Income from career; fulfillment of professional desires |
| 12th | Vyaya | Expenses, foreign lands, isolation, spirituality | Foreign employment; behind-the-scenes work; hospitals |

- **7th House**: Business, partnerships

## Lords of Each House in a Chart

The lord (ruling planet) of a house is determined by which rashi occupies that house.

**Example**: If the 10th house has Capricorn (Makar), its lord is Saturn (Shani). Saturn's strength, placement, and dignity will largely determine career outcomes.

## Key Dignity States of Planets

| State | Meaning | Career Impact |
|-------|---------|---------------|
| Exalted (Uccha) | Planet at peak strength in a specific sign | Excellent career results in that planet's domain |
| Own Sign (Swakshetra) | Planet in the sign it rules | Strong and stable career |
| Friendly Sign (Mitra) | Planet in a friendly sign | Generally good results |
| Neutral Sign | Planet in a neutral sign | Moderate results |
| Enemy Sign (Shatru) | Planet in an enemy's sign | Reduced effectiveness |
| Debilitated (Neecha) | Planet at weakest point | Challenges; cancellation possible with Neechabhanga |

## Reading Planet Degrees in a Chart

In a Lagna (birth) chart, each planet is shown with:
- Its abbreviation (Su = Sun, Mo = Moon, Ma = Mars, Me = Mercury, Ju = Jupiter, Ve = Venus, Sa = Saturn, Ra = Rahu, Ke = Ketu, As = Ascendant)
- Its degree number (e.g., Su₁₉ means Sun at 19°)
- "R" suffix = Retrograde (e.g., Ve₀₄R = Venus retrograde at 4°)
