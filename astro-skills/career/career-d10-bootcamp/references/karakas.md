# Karakas — Profession & Industry by Planet

Source: Astro Arun Pandit — The Career Compass (D10 Bootcamp)

---

## SUN (Surya)

**Industries:**
- Government and administration
- Politics and leadership roles
- Defense (Army, Navy, Air Force)
- Goldsmiths and jewelry design
- Power generation (solar energy, electricity)
- Agriculture, especially dealing with grains

**Professions:**
Leaders, Politicians, Government Officials, Doctors, Goldsmiths, CEOs

---

## MOON (Chandra)

**Industries:**
- Dairy, milk, and food products
- Water-related industries (fisheries, beverages, irrigation, shipping)
- Hospitality (hotels, catering, spas)
- Art and crafts, including textiles and interior design
- Silver and pearl trade
- Pharmaceuticals and healing professions
- Astrology and psychology
- Pet-related work

**Professions:**
Nurses, caretakers, chefs, interior designers, maritime professionals

---

## MARS (Mangal)

**Industries:**
- Engineering (civil, mechanical, aerospace)
- Military and police forces
- Real estate and construction
- Sports and athletics-related industries
- Metallurgy (iron, steel)
- Weapons and defense equipment manufacturing
- Surgery (especially orthopedic and trauma-related)
- Ayurvedic medicine, dry fruits, sweets, handicraft

**Professions:**
Soldiers, Athletes, Engineers, Surgeons, Real Estate Developers

---

## MERCURY (Budh)

**Industries:**
- Information technology and software
- Media, journalism, and publishing
- Accounting, finance, and auditing
- Education and coaching
- Trade and commerce
- Telecommunications
- Mathematics and statistics
- Writing and literature

**Professions:**
Writers, Accountants, IT Professionals, Teachers, Traders, Journalists, Analysts

---

## JUPITER (Guru/Brihaspati)

**Industries:**
- Education and academics
- Law and judiciary
- Finance, banking, and investment
- Spiritual institutions and religious organizations
- Consulting and advisory services
- Philosophy and higher learning

**Professions:**
Judges, Lawyers, Teachers, Professors, Financial Advisors, Priests, Consultants

---

## VENUS (Shukra)

**Industries:**
- Entertainment (film, music, theater)
- Fashion and luxury goods
- Beauty and cosmetics
- Hospitality and tourism
- Art galleries and creative industries
- Automobile industry (especially luxury vehicles)
- Perfumes and fragrances
- Gemstone and jewelry trade (ornamental)

**Professions:**
Actors, Musicians, Fashion Designers, Interior Decorators, Hotel Managers, Artists

---

## SATURN (Shani)

**Industries:**
- Labor-intensive industries (construction, mining)
- Agriculture and farming
- Judiciary and law enforcement
- Iron, steel, and heavy machinery
- Oil and petroleum
- Waste management and sanitation
- Social work and NGOs
- Real estate (land dealings)

**Professions:**
Laborers, Farmers, Judges, Factory Workers, Social Workers, Land Dealers

---

## RAHU

**Industries:**
- Information technology (especially cutting-edge, AI)
- Foreign companies and multinational corporations
- Unconventional or taboo industries (gambling, liquor, chemicals)
- Media, social media, and digital content
- Aviation
- Research and innovation
- Export/import and foreign trade

**Professions:**
Entrepreneurs, Foreign-based Professionals, Researchers, Social Media Influencers, Pilots

---

## KETU

**Industries:**
- Spirituality and occult sciences
- Medical research and alternative medicine
- Intelligence services (espionage, investigation)
- Astrology, tantra, mysticism
- Programming and deep technical research
- Isolation-based work (writing, analysis)

**Professions:**
Astrologers, Spiritual Healers, Researchers, Intelligence Officers, Mystics
