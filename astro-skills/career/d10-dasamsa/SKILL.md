# D10 (Dasamsa) Chart Skill

## Overview

The D10, also known as Dasamsa, is the 10th divisional chart in Vedic astrology. It is
the primary chart for analysing career, profession, achievements, authority, and public
life. Always reference this skill for any D10-related query.

For detailed house meanings, planetary indicators, and 10th lord placements, load the
relevant reference file from `references/`.

---

## What the D10 Chart Reveals

- Type of career, job stability, and overall accomplishments
- How a person is perceived in their professional sphere
- Insights into work ethic and sense of responsibility
- Periods of career advancement or challenges
- Authority and leadership potential

---

## Step-by-Step Analysis Framework

### 1. Identify Key Indicators (read in this order)

1. **Ascendant (Lagna) of D10** — Overall approach to career and public image
2. **10th House Lord of D1 (natal chart) placed in D10** — Primary career signifier
3. **Placement of D10 Lagna lord in D10** — How the native projects in their field
4. **Strength of 10th House in D10 and its lord's position** — Career strength

### 2. Check Planetary Placements

Consult `references/planetary-indicators.md` for Sun, Moon, and Saturn rules.

**Quick reference:**
- **Sun in Kendra (angles)** → Government/authority career with high status
- **Sun in 6th/8th/12th** → Problems from government or authority figures
- **Strong Saturn** → Support from subordinates, potential political career
- **Weak Saturn** → Conflicts with subordinates
- **Strong Moon** → Zeal and enthusiasm for work
- **Weak Moon** → Inferiority complex, lack of drive
- **Raj Yoga in D10** → High status/position in profession
- **Dhana Yoga in D10** → Wealth from multiple professions

### 3. Evaluate Planet Positions by House Type

**Kendra (Angle) Houses — 1st, 4th, 7th, 10th:**
- Most powerful for delivering career results
- Even malefics give support when placed here
- Order of strength: 10th > 7th > 4th > 1st

**Trikona (Trine) Houses — 5th and 9th:**
- Generally auspicious if unafflicted
- Note: 5th is 8th from 10th; 9th is 12th from 10th — afflictions here bring career
  obstacles or sudden changes

### 4. Determine Career Orientation: Service vs. Business

Consult `references/house-attributes.md` for the 6th vs. 7th house comparison:
- **6th house stronger** → Salaried service/employment orientation
- **7th house stronger** → Business/partnership orientation

### 5. Apply 10th Lord Placement Rules

Consult `references/tenth-lord-placements.md` for all 12 house interpretations.

### 6. Check Ruling Deities (Dashamsha Deities)

Consult `references/ruling-deities.md` for the deity table based on degrees in odd/even
signs and their associated directions, ruling planets, and alternate versions.
