# D10 House Attributes & Career Orientation

- Beginning of Career, Perception of Career, Strength of Karma
- Ascendant of D10 represents the native's overall approach and projection in career

- Resources and Speech available for career

- Creativity and communication skills in career

### House 4 — Comfort from Profession

### House 5 — Status & Wellwishers
- Status in professional field

### House 8 — Hidden & Problematic Activities

### House 9 — Rightful Path

### House 10 — Status & Boss

### House 11 — Gains from Career

### House 12 — End & Foreign Connections

## Decoding Career Orientation: Service vs. Business

### Method: Compare 6th House vs. 7th House Strength

| Factor | 6th House (Service) | 7th House (Business) |
|--------|--------------------|-----------------------|
| **Meaning** | Employment, serving others | Partnerships, independent trade |
| **Stronger = ?** | Salaried job, government service | Self-employment, business |
| **Key planets** | 6th lord strength, planets in 6th | 7th lord strength, planets in 7th |
| **Supporting factors** | Sun in Kendra (govt job), strong Saturn | Mercury/Venus strong, 7th lord in angle |

### Evaluation Steps
1. Count planets in 6th vs. 7th house
2. Check whether 6th lord or 7th lord is stronger (own sign, exalted, in Kendra)
3. Check aspects from benefics or malefics on each house
4. If roughly equal → mixed career (service with side business, or freelance)

### Additional Signals
- **Sun prominent** → Government or authority-based career
- **Mercury/Venus prominent** → Trade, communication, creative business
- **Saturn prominent** → Service, discipline-based career or politics
- **Jupiter prominent** → Teaching, advisory, consultancy roles
- **Mars prominent** → Technical, engineering, military, or competitive careers
