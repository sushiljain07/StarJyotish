# Planetary Indicators in D10 Chart

## Sun — Authority & Government

| Placement | Interpretation |
|-----------|---------------|
| Sun in Kendra (1st, 4th, 7th, 10th) | Government jobs with high status and income |
| Sun in 6th house | Problems from government/authority |
| Sun in 8th house | Hidden conflicts with authority, career instability |
| Sun in 12th house | Problems from government, foreign service possible |

**Sun = Authority, Government, Father figures, Public image, Soul's purpose in career**

---

## Saturn — Discipline, Service & Workers

| Placement | Interpretation |
|-----------|---------------|
| Strongly placed Saturn (own sign, exalted, Kendra) | Support from subordinates and workers; career in politics possible |
| Weakly placed Saturn (debilitated, 6th/8th/12th, afflicted) | Conflicts with subordinates, labour disputes |

**Saturn = Discipline, hard work, masses, subordinates, political career, longevity of career**

---

## Moon — Mental Attitude Towards Work

| Placement | Interpretation |
|-----------|---------------|
| 10th lord from Moon in D10 — strong | Native has zeal and enthusiasm for work |
| 10th lord from Moon in D10 — weak | Native develops inferiority complex about career |
| Strongly placed Moon | High motivation, emotional fulfilment from career |
| Weakly placed Moon | Lack of enthusiasm, self-doubt in professional sphere |

**Key rule:** Check the 10th house from the Moon's position in D10 to assess mental
attitude toward profession and karma.

---

## Raj Yoga in D10

- Formed by lords of Kendra and Trikona houses joining together (conjunction/mutual aspect/exchange)
- **Outcome:** High position and status in profession, recognition, honour
- Even a weak Raj Yoga in D10 gives career elevation during its Dasha period

---

## Dhana Yoga in D10

- Formed by 2nd/11th lords connecting with benefic planets or each other
- **Outcome:** Wealth from multiple professions; more than one income source
- Indicates native may work in multiple fields simultaneously

---

## Kendra (Angle) Planets — Career Power Houses

Houses: **1st, 4th, 7th, 10th**

- Planets here are most powerful to deliver career results
- Even natural malefics (Saturn, Mars, Rahu, Ketu) give support when placed in Kendra
- **Strength order:** 10th > 7th > 4th > 1st

**Why Kendra malefics help:** They provide drive, ambition, and ability to overcome
obstacles in the career arena, even if they create some tension.

---

## Trine (Trikona) Planets — Luck & Dharma in Career

Houses: **5th and 9th**

- Planets in 5th and 9th (and their lords) are auspicious **if unafflicted**
- **Important nuance:**
  - 5th house = 8th from 10th house → afflictions here bring sudden career changes or obstacles
  - 9th house = 12th from 10th house → afflictions here bring losses or end-of-career phases
- Unafflicted benefics in 5th or 9th bring good fortune, luck, and dharmic career path
