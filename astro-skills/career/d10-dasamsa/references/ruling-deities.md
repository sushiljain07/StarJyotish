# Ruling Deities of Dashamsha (D10)

Each 3° segment within a sign corresponds to a specific deity, direction, and ruling
planet. The assignment differs based on whether the sign is **Odd (1,3,5,7,9,11)** or
**Even (2,4,6,8,10,12)**.

## How to Use This Table

1. Find the **exact degree** of the planet or Lagna you are examining in D10
2. Identify whether it falls in an **Odd** or **Even** sign
3. Match the degree range to find the **deity**, **direction**, and **ruling planet**
4. Use the deity and direction for:
   - Spiritual remedies (propitiate the deity)
   - Understanding the flavour/theme of that planetary energy in career
   - Directional analysis (favourable/unfavourable directions for career)

## Note on Two Versions

The table provides two versions of ruling planets — **primary** and **alternate**. Both
traditions exist in Vedic astrology literature. Use the primary version as default; the
alternate may be applied for cross-verification or when the primary interpretation seems
inconsistent with the chart.
