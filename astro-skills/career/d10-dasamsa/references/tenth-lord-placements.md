# 10th House Lord Placements in D10 Chart

This file describes what it means when the 10th House Lord (of the D10 chart) is placed
in each of the 12 houses of the D10 chart.

---

## House 1
- **Good placement overall**
- Native is learned, famous, and wealthy
- If afflicted, it can make the native cunning

## House 2
- Speech, food industry, or family business may become the focus
- Native constantly works to improve wealth
- If afflicted, can cause loss of wealth and status

## House 3
- Native is self-made and works hard for success
- Communication, writing, and short travels are part of work
- Malefic association gives courage and capacity; however, malefic aspects are not desirable
- Could be in a transferable job or may change jobs frequently

## House 4
- Native prospers in own land and has authority
- Native is generous and does charity
- Native can pursue politics as a career, especially if associated with Saturn and the 9th house/lord

## House 5
- Native is famous, learned, and intelligent
- Native will not be able to sustain a job for longer periods
- Child of the native is famous and highly placed in society

## House 6
- Native will have a service and servitude approach towards the job
- Native depends on luck for furthering the career
- This house is related to problems of society (disease, debt, enemy), and the native earns their living through these services

## House 7
- Native has strong business skills
- Native may have an association with foreigners or travel as a part of their profession

## House 8
- Native may face challenges and uncertainty in their professional career
- If Lagna is strong, then the native achieves great positions with a lot of struggles
- With benefic association, the native may reach high positions quickly

## House 9
- **Raj Yoga**
- Native will be wealthy and attain a high position by sheer luck
- Native may change jobs frequently or may be in transferable jobs

## House 10
- Gives distinction in work
- Native gets honor and fame
- If afflicted, the native gets famous for the wrong reasons

## House 11
- **Auspicious placement in all undertakings**
- Native gets success with least efforts
- Native gives employment to many people
- Native has more than one profession at a time

## House 12
- Native may have a foreign-related profession or profession related to hospitals, jails, or solitary/secluded places
- Association with the 12th lord gives unsuccessful career
