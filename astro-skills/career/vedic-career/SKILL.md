# Vedic Career Astrology (D-10 Analysis)

This skill covers career analysis using Vedic astrology principles — from basic chart reading to timing predictions. Content sourced from the course career astrology course notes.

## 1. Job vs Business — How to Determine

### Step 1: Ashtakvarga Analysis
- Compare **6th house score** (employment) vs **7th house score** (business/partnership)
- Higher 6th house score → inclined toward **job/service**
- Higher 7th house score → inclined toward **business/self-employment**
- Similar scores → both paths are viable; use planetary relationships to decide

### Step 2: House Lord Connections (D-1 Chart)
- **6th lord aspects/conjuncts 2nd lord** → job is primary income source
- **7th lord aspects/conjuncts 2nd lord** → business is more favorable
- **Both 6th & 7th lords connect with 2nd lord** → use Ashtakvarga points to decide
- **No clear connection** → rely purely on Ashtakvarga scores

### Step 3: D-10 Chart Confirmation
- Check 6th house and its lord in D-10 for job indications
- Check 7th house and its lord in D-10 for business indications
- Cross-verify with 11th house lord connections in D-10

## 2. Identifying Career Nature — Planetary Combinations

### 1st Mahadasha Foundation
The first Mahadasha/Antardasha planet shapes the native's professional style permanently:

| Planet | Career Style / Industry |
|--------|------------------------|
| Rahu | Communication, foreign connections, logistics, magnifying roles |
| Venus | Creative/luxury industries, female manager, visually appealing workplace |
| Jupiter | Teaching, finance, mentoring, leadership, guiding roles |
| Saturn | Real estate, hard labor, serving subordinates; strong Saturn = more responsibility |
| Saturn + Mercury | Communication-heavy professions |
| Saturn + Moon | Client relationship building, nurturing workplace environment |

### 4th House from Sun → Natural Abilities
Count 4 signs forward from the Sun's placement:
- The sign's ruling planet reveals innate talents and strengths for career growth
- **Example:** Sun in Aries → 4th house falls in Cancer (Moon-ruled) → emotional intelligence, counseling, healthcare, teaching
- **Example:** Sun in Libra → 4th from Sun is Capricorn (Saturn-ruled) → management, administration, finance, engineering

### 10th House from Saturn → Karma & Stability
Count 10 signs forward from Saturn's placement:
- Reveals the career path that brings long-term stability and karmic fulfillment
- **Example:** Saturn in Virgo → 10th from Saturn is Gemini (Mercury-ruled) → journalism, writing, data analysis, consulting
- **Example:** Saturn in Scorpio → 10th from Saturn is Leo (Sun-ruled) → politics, corporate leadership, government positions

## 3. Specific Planetary Placements & Careers

### Saturn & Jupiter Interchange
- **Saturn in 10th house** → pursue Jupiter's domains: education, wisdom, spirituality, teaching
- **Jupiter in 10th house** → pursue Saturn's domains: administration, government, legal work, discipline-driven fields

### Mercury's Career Influence
- **Mercury in 6th house** → agriculture, printing, stationery (precision and analysis)
- **Mercury in 10th house** → wide business opportunities; thrives in structured trade; Shermukhi properties favorable

### Mars's Career Influence
- **Mars in 1st house** → iron, wood, construction, land-related work
- **Mars in 2nd house** → beauty, creativity, design (Moon + Venus fields)
- **Mars in 9th house** → restaurant/food industry; fire-related professions

### Saturn's Role in Industry & Law
- **Saturn in 7th house** → iron, steel, heavy machinery, alcohol industry
- **Saturn–Sun conjunction within 10–12°** → powerful indicator for law (judges, lawyers, legal advisors); also government jobs
- **Saturn + Sun in 1st house** → gold, silver, fire-related industries; restaurants, metallurgy, jewelry

### Notable Combinations
| Combination | Indication |
|-------------|-----------|
| Mercury + Saturn in 2nd house | Iron-related professions, technical/strategic roles |
| Mars + Mercury | Medical field — surgeons, doctors (surgical precision + analytics) |
| Jupiter + Venus in 2nd house | Real estate, land, infrastructure — but avoid Jupiter careers if Jupiter is in 2nd house of D-1 |
| Jupiter in 2nd house (D-1) | Pursue Venus domains: glamour, luxury, fashion, diamond industry |
| Retrograde key career planet | Unconventional career path (e.g., retrograde Jupiter → unique teaching methods) |

## 4. Timing of Career Events

### First Job
Primary trigger: **Dasha of 12th house lord** (D-1 or D-10) — main, sub, or sub-sub period.

Additional timing rules (any of these can trigger first employment):
1. Mahadasha/Antardasha of **10th lord, 6th lord** of D-1 or D-10, or **Saturn** begins
2. **Saturn or Jupiter transits over** or aspects the 10th house/10th lord of D-1 or D-10
3. **Saturn aspects the Lagna** or 10th house of D-1 or D-10
4. If Saturn connects with 12th house → watch **Sade Sati** (7.5-year Saturn transit over natal Moon)

### Promotions & Career Advancement
- Planet associated with **10th house becomes active** in its dasha → career growth, leadership
- **Lagna lord + exalted planet** in D-10 entering dasha → promotion opportunity
- Favorable dasha of 10th, 6th, or 11th house + **Jupiter transiting 10th/11th house or their lord**
- **Transiting Jupiter over natal Sun** (D-1 or D-10) → favorable for promotion
- **Sun transiting 10th house or forming Raj Yoga** → enhanced status and authority

### Difficult Career Phases
Key planets to watch: **Saturn, Rahu, Ketu**

| Transit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Saturn connecting with 10th house lord or occupying 10th | Demands career change; resistance causes stagnation; results improve after 6–8 months |
| Saturn transiting 10th house | Delays, recognition only after hard work |
| Sun in Saturn's sign | Conflicts with superiors |
| Saturn conjunct natal Sun | Ego conflicts with authority, thoughts of resignation, father-related issues |
| Saturn in 8th from Moon/Ascendant | Unexpected obstacles, professional instability |
| Saturn in 4th or 10th from Ascendant | Career difficulties |
| Rahu/Ketu transit | Illusions — "I'm better suited for another job"; if Jupiter aspects, relief is significant |
| Mars transiting 10th house | Conflicts, aggression, impulsive decisions |
| Jupiter transiting 6th, 8th, or 12th | Fewer career opportunities, slower growth |

### Job Switches & Transfers
Frequent job changes: **Saturn or Moon in 6th or 10th house** (D-1 or D-10)

| Dasha | Likely Event |
|-------|-------------|
| 6th house dasha (D-1 or D-10) | Positive career change aligned with goals |
| 8th house dasha (D-1 or D-10) | Unexpected/forced career shift, layoffs |
| 12th house dasha (D-1 or D-10) | Beginning of professional journey |
| 3rd house lord dasha (D-10) | Retirement or switching industries |
| 6th/10th/12th lord dasha (D-1) + Saturn/Rahu transiting 10th | Job loss likely |

Additional transit triggers for job change:
- Saturn transiting 12th or 1st house → desire to withdraw from work
- Rahu–Ketu axis on 4th–10th houses or aspecting 12th house
- Transit of Ketu or Saturn over natal Saturn

### New Job Opportunities
- Active dasha of **11th house lord + 10th house** or **11th house + 5th house** (D-1 or D-10)
- Sudden job loss: **8th house of D-10 connects with active dasha period**

### Foreign Career Opportunities
- **Rahu, Saturn, or Ketu in 12th house** → foreign travel/settlement
- **Rahu or Ketu in 4th house** → relocation abroad
- **12th house lord in 9th, 10th, or 11th house** → favorable for long-term foreign career

## 5. Case Study Application Method

When analyzing a real chart, follow this sequence:

1. **Determine Job vs Business** → Ashtakvarga 6th vs 7th + house lord connections in D-1 and D-10
2. **Identify career nature** → 1st Mahadasha planet, 4th from Sun, 10th from Saturn
3. **Spot prominent combinations** → check planetary conjunctions and aspects
4. **Time career events** → map dasha periods to the rules above; verify with transits of Jupiter and Saturn
5. **Cross-verify D-1 and D-10** → events are confirmed when both charts corroborate
