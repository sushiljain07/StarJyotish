## Sports Career Combinations

- **5th house** → indoor sports (badminton, chess)
- **6th house** → outdoor sports (cricket, tennis)
- **Strong Mars** is essential for any sports career
- Analyze **5th–10th house relationship** or **6th–10th house relationship**
- Look at **trine planets** (1st, 5th, 9th houses)
- For **chess and brain-intensive games**: Mercury and Rahu must be well-placed

Key checks:
1. Mars strength in D-1 and D-10
2. Connection between 5th/6th house and 10th house
3. Mercury + Rahu placement for strategic/mental sports

## Real Estate Career Combinations

- **Saturn + Mars together** or **mutual aspect** = strong real estate combination
  - Mars = karaka of Land, Buildings, Action
  - Saturn = karaka of Construction, Long-term Assets, Stability
- When positively placed → property gains, land acquisition, real estate success
- When negatively placed or afflicted by malefics → delays, legal issues, struggles

Additional indicators:
- **Jupiter + Venus in 2nd house** → land-related professions (real estate, infrastructure, property dealings)
  - Exception: If Jupiter is in 2nd house of D-1, native should avoid Jupiter's domains and focus on Venus domains instead

## Medical Profession Combinations

Key planets: **Mars, Saturn, Rahu**
- Mars = primary karaka of surgery and medical intervention

Required conditions:
1. **10th house and 6th house** of Lagna chart (D-1) **AND** D-10 chart both connect with **Mars**
2. Along with **Saturn or Rahu**

Strong Mars with either Saturn (discipline, chronic conditions) or Rahu (unusual/foreign medical fields) in connection with 10th and 6th houses indicates medical inclination.

Notable: **Mars + Mercury combination** → particularly favorable for surgeons and doctors (surgical precision + analytical intelligence). This combination is challenging for childbirth matters but highly favorable for the medical field.

- **Saturn–Sun conjunction** (within 10–12°) → judges, lawyers, legal advisors, government jobs
- **Jupiter in 10th house** → authority, structure-driven public roles
- **Saturn in Scorpio** with 10th falling in Leo → corporate leadership, politics, government
- These natives are known for **dedication and trustworthiness** over widespread fame

## Communication / Media / Writing Combinations

- **Mercury in 10th house** → wide business opportunities, any type of trade

- **Saturn–Mercury combination** in profession → communication-heavy career
