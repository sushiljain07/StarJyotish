## Remedies by Planet

### Saturn Remedies
- Avoid alcohol and non-vegetarian food
- Remain highly cautious in actions
- Chant **Vishnu Sahastranama** regularly
- Engage in mountain trekking for Devi Darshan

### Rahu Remedies
- Donations at a **Maa Durga temple**
- Work in an MNC or go abroad
- Take a bath in the Ganges
- *(Note: When Rahu transits 10th house → sudden challenges, scandals, career disruptions)*
- *(Note: When Ketu transits 10th house → detachment, disinterest, unstable professional phase)*

### Ketu Remedies
When Ketu transits over Saturn (detachment from work):
- Worship **Lord Ganesha**
- Chant **Ganesh Atharvashirsha**
- Make donations at a well-known Ganesh temple
- Feed fish (aids in letting go of attachments)
- Feed stray dogs

**Ritual for Emotional Detachment / Desire to Leave Job:**
- Take dry fruits in both hands
- Go to a river or sea
- Say aloud the things you want to let go
- Perform **only once** and **only on a Wednesday**

### Mercury Remedies
Mercury in 8th or 9th house needs strengthening:
- **Annual donation** to an orphan girl child
- **Once-in-lifetime ritual:** Fill a copper vessel with green gram (moong dal); bury it at midnight on a Wednesday; ensure no one asks you anything during the process

### Financial Burden / Loan Remedies
- Chant **Ganesh Rinn Mukti Stotram**
- For significant loans: perform **seva (selfless service)** at a Gurudwara
- On **Chaturdashi**: perform remedies related to Ketu or Lord Ganesha

### Remedy for Aspiring Astrologers
- Chant the **Gayatri Mantra** daily
- Offer water to the Sun God (Surya Arghya) every morning

## Rudraksha Recommendations for Career

| Mukhi | Benefit |
|-------|---------|
| **6 Mukhi** | Resolves job-related problems; brings career stability and steady income |
| **8 Mukhi** | Overcomes sudden challenges; supports unconventional career paths; enhances competitiveness |
| **10 Mukhi** | Protection against stress, negativity, and workplace politics |
| **11 Mukhi** | Associated with Lord Hanuman; ideal for handling multiple roles, crisis management |
| **14 Mukhi** (Shani Rudraksha) | Ideal for business professionals dealing with large financial transactions |

## Puja and Mantra Chanting

All pujas should be performed under supervision of an experienced pundit/priest.

| Puja | Purpose | Mantra |
|------|---------|--------|
| **Ganesh Puja** | Remove career obstacles; smooth progress | — |
| **Navgraha Poojan** | Balance planetary influences affecting career stability | — |
| **Lakshmi Kuber Puja** | Attract wealth; support professional growth | *Om Shreem Mahalakshmaye Namah* |
| **Saturn Puja** | Resolve delays, obstacles, Saturn-related challenges | *Om Sham Shanicharaya Namah* |

**Saturn Mantra Instructions:**
- Chant 108 times daily
- Face **northeast direction**
- Sit on a **kush asana** (no one should touch your mala or asana)
- Continue until completing **1.25 lakh (125,000) chants**
- Fix a specific time and follow it consistently
