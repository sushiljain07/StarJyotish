# D7 (Saptamsha) Chart — Complete Reading Guide

The **D7 or Saptamsha** is the most specific divisional chart for studying children, progeny, and fertility in Vedic astrology. It is the 7th harmonic division of each sign.

---

## When to Use the D7 Chart

- After the D1 (Rashi) chart shows a promise of children.
- To confirm timing and nature of childbirth.
- To study the destiny of children born to the native.
- When D1 shows delay — D7 tells whether it's temporary or karmic.
- For IVF, adoption, or unusual birth circumstances.

---

## How D7 is Calculated

Each sign (30°) is divided into 7 equal parts of 4°17' each. The planet falls in one of 7 sub-divisions, and this determines its D7 position.

## Step-by-Step D7 Reading

### Step 1: Assess the D7 Lagna
- A strong Lagna (with benefics or powerful Lagna lord) = good prognosis.
- Malefics on Lagna without benefic aspect = challenges in having children.

### Step 2: Check the 5th House in D7
- Benefics in 5th of D7 = children come with relative ease.
- Malefics in 5th of D7 (especially Saturn, Rahu, Ketu) = delays or complications.
- Empty 5th but with strong 5th lord = children possible with effort.

### Step 3: Locate Jupiter in D7
- Jupiter in Kendra (1, 4, 7, 10) or Trikona (1, 5, 9) of D7 = positive.
- Jupiter in 6th, 8th, or 12th of D7 = weakened; delay likely.
- Jupiter retrograde in D7 = past-life debt around children to be resolved.

### Step 4: Check the 5th Lord of D7
- Is the 5th lord of D7 in a good house?
- Is it aspected by benefics?
- Is it conjunct a malefic without relief?

### Step 5: Cross-Reference with D1 and D9
- D1 shows the promise.
- D9 confirms the soul-level agreement.
- D7 shows the physical/material manifestation.
- All three should align for a confident prediction.

## D7 and the Gender of Children

- **Sun dominant in D7 5th** → Male children likely
- **Moon/Venus dominant in D7 5th** → Female children likely
- **Mercury in D7 5th** → Neutral / twins possible
- **Jupiter in D7 5th** → Multiple children of both genders

*Note: Gender prediction is approximate and not the primary purpose of D7 analysis.*

## D60 (Shashtiamsha) — Supplementary for Twins and Special Cases

When twins are indicated in D1 and D7, cross-check with D60:
- Dual signs (Gemini, Sagittarius, Pisces) strong in D60's 5th.
- Mercury's strength in D60.
- Jupiter's aspect on D60 Lagna or 5th.
