## The Rule of 12-9-12 (Kundli Reading Framework)

In a North Indian chart:
- Houses are represented by **Roman numerals** and are **always fixed**.
- Rashis (zodiac signs) rotate based on the Lagna (Ascendant).
- The numbers inside the chart (in green) represent the Rashi numbers placed in each house.

This means: **House I ≠ Rashi 1 (Aries) always.** The Lagna determines which Rashi falls in which house.
