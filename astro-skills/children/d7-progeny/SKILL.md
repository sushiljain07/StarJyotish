# D7 Progeny Astrology Skill

Comprehensive Vedic astrology system for evaluating progeny potential using Beej Sphutak (male fertility), Kshetra Sphutak (female fertility), divisional chart analysis, and special birth considerations.

## Beej Sphutak (Male Fertility)

**Purpose:** Evaluates a male's generative/reproductive capacity and the health of seed (sperm).

**Formula:**
> Beej Sphutak = Longitude of **Jupiter + Sun + Venus** (all converted to degrees)

### Calculation Steps
1. Convert each planet's sign + degree to absolute degrees (see Sign Degree Table below).
2. Add all three absolute longitudes.
3. If total > 360°, subtract 360°.
4. Divide result by 30 to find the sign; remainder is the degree within that sign.
5. Identify the Nakshatra using the Nakshatra Degree Table.
6. Check this point in D1, D7, and D9 charts.

### Good Indicators (Fertility Potential High)
- Falls in an **odd sign** in D1, D9 & D7
- Falls in **fertile signs** (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces, Taurus)
- In friendly signs to Jupiter or Sun
- Falls in **benefic nakshatras** (Rohini, Pushya, Anuradha)
- **Unafflicted** — not conjunct/aspected by Rahu, Ketu, Saturn, Mars
- Lies in **Kendra** (1st, 4th, 7th, 10th) or **Trikona** (1st, 5th, 9th) houses

### Problematic Indicators (Infertility / Delays)
- Falls in **barren signs** (Gemini, Leo, Virgo)
- Conjunct/aspected by malefics (Rahu, Ketu, Saturn, Mars)
- Lies in **6th, 8th, or 12th** house from Lagna or 5th house lord
- **Combust or retrograde** influence involved
- Falls in nakshatras of misfortune: **Moola, Ashlesha, Jyeshtha**

## Kshetra Sphutak (Female Fertility / Womb)

**Purpose:** Evaluates the womb's fertility — the ability to conceive and carry a child.

*(Kshetra = क्षेत्र = Field/Womb; Sphutak = स्फुट = Calculated Point)*

**Formula:**
> Kshetra Sphutak = Longitude of **Moon + Mars + Jupiter** (all converted to degrees)

### Calculation Steps
Same 5-step process as Beej Sphutak (see above), using Moon, Mars, and Jupiter.

### Good Indicators (Fertility Potential High)
- Falls in an **even sign** in D1, D9 & D7
- Falls in **fertile signs**
- **Aspected or conjoined** by Jupiter, Venus, or Moon
- Falls in **benefic nakshatras** (Rohini, Pushya, Anuradha)
- **Unafflicted** by Rahu, Ketu, Saturn, Mars
- Lies in **Kendra or Trikona** houses

### Problematic Indicators (Infertility / Delays)
- Falls in **barren signs**
- Conjunct/aspected by malefics
- Lies in **6th, 8th, or 12th** house from Lagna or 5th house lord
- **Combust or retrograde** influence involved
- Falls in misfortune nakshatras: **Moola, Ashlesha, Jyeshtha**

> **Key Difference:** Beej Sphutak should fall in an *odd* sign; Kshetra Sphutak should fall in an *even* sign for positive progeny promise.

## Interpretation Framework

After calculating and placing the Sphutak, interpret in this order:

1. **Sign quality** — Fertile (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces, Taurus) vs. Barren (Gemini, Leo, Virgo)?
2. **Odd/Even rule** — Beej = odd sign ✓; Kshetra = even sign ✓
3. **Nakshatra quality** — Benefic (Rohini, Pushya, Anuradha) vs. Difficult (Moola, Ashlesha, Jyeshtha)?
4. **Afflictions** — Any malefic conjunction or aspect (Rahu, Ketu, Saturn, Mars)?
5. **House placement** — Kendra/Trikona = supportive; 6th/8th/12th = challenging
6. **D7 chart** — Which house does the Sphutak sign fall in? Aspects received?
7. **D9 chart** — Same analysis for deeper karmic layer
8. **Jupiter's strength** — Always note Jupiter's condition; a strong Jupiter can mitigate many afflictions and often indicates successful conception even with medical help

**Key synthesis rule:** Afflictions indicate delays or need for medical intervention, but a strong Jupiter can still deliver a positive outcome. The nature of affliction often hints at the *type* of challenge (Mars = surgical/inflammatory; Rahu = unconventional methods like IVF; Saturn = delay).

## Case Study Template

### Beej Sphutak Example (Male, DOB: 15-10-1988, Delhi)
- Venus (Leo 18°) = 120 + 18 = **138°**
- Sun (Virgo 28°) = 150 + 28 = **178°**
- Jupiter (Taurus 11°) = 30 + 11 = **41°**
- Total = 138 + 178 + 41 = **357°** (within 360°, no subtraction needed)
- 357 ÷ 30 = 11 full signs, remainder = 27° → **12th sign = Pisces at 27°**
- Pisces = fertile sign ✓; 27° Pisces = **Revati Nakshatra** (Mercury-ruled)
- Affliction: Mars aspects from 7th house → surgical intervention likely
- Support: Jupiter strong → successful outcome (twins via IVF)

### Kshetra Sphutak Example (Female, DOB: 16-08-1996, Bulandshehr)
- Mars (Gemini 20°) = 60 + 20 = **80°**
- Moon (Leo 24°) = 120 + 24 = **144°**
- Jupiter (Sagittarius 14°) = 240 + 14 = **254°**
- Total = 80 + 144 + 254 = **478°** → 478 - 360 = **118°**
- 118 ÷ 30 = 3 full signs, remainder = 28° → **4th sign = Cancer at 28°**
- Cancer = fertile, watery sign ✓; 28° Cancer = **Ashlesha Nakshatra**
- Cancer in 6th house (dusthana) in D1 — but blank, no malefic aspect → reduced intensity
- D7: Cancer receives Jupiter aspect → highly beneficial for fertility
- D9: Cancer in 8th house, aspected by Ketu → hidden karmic layers
