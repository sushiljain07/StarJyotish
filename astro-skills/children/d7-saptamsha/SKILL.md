# D7 Saptamsha – Complete Reference Skill

This skill covers the full D7 (Saptamsha) chart system as taught in classical Vedic astrology,
including house significations, Sapt Ras theory, Saptarishi origins, Lagna lord results,
progeny combinations, and timing.

---

## 1. What is the D7 Chart?

The **D7 or Saptamsha** is a divisional (Varga) chart derived from the natal (D1) chart. Each
zodiac sign is divided into **7 equal parts of ~4°17' each**. It is the primary chart for
judging everything related to **children and progeny** — their birth, nature, timing, health,
karma, and the native's relationship with them.

## 3. The Seven Divisions (Sapt Ras Theory)

Each zodiac sign is divided into 7 parts. The **sequence differs for odd and even signs**:

| Division | Degrees | Odd Sign | Even Sign |
|----------|---------|----------|-----------|
| 1st | 0° – 4°17'08" | Kshara | Shuddha Jala |
| 2nd | 4°17'08" – 8°34'17" | Ksheera | Madhya |
| 3rd | 8°34'17" – 12°51'25" | Dadhi | Ikshu |
| 4th | 12°51'25" – 17°08'34" | Ajya | Ajya |
| 5th | 17°08'34" – 21°25'42" | Ikshu | Dadhi |
| 6th | 21°25'42" – 25°42'51" | Madhya | Ksheera |
| 7th | 25°42'51" – 30° | Shuddha Jala | Kshara |

**How to use**: Identify which division the D1 Lagna falls in → that Ras describes the native's
karmic quality with their children.

**Odd signs**: Aries, Gemini, Leo, Libra, Sagittarius, Aquarius
**Even signs**: Taurus, Cancer, Virgo, Scorpio, Capricorn, Pisces

### Qualities of Each Ras

| Ras | Symbol | Quality |
|-----|--------|---------|
| **Kshara** | Salt/Alkali | Hardship; stress, humiliation, or sorrow through children; path of struggle that can lead to strength; ethical dilemmas |
| **Ksheera** | Milk | Purity, nourishment, sattvic qualities; children uplift parents emotionally & socially; dynamic energy, healing, spiritual strength |
| **Dadhi** | Curd | Individuality and consistency; children carry unique identity, bring joy and pride; bold personality, emotional depth |
| **Ajya** | Ghee | Spiritual refinement and richness; children respectful, devout, bring wealth or prosperity; some restlessness |
| **Ikshu** | Sugarcane Juice | Sweet and vibrant; children affectionate and strong; outwardly dynamic, inwardly soft — if over-indulged, may become arrogant |
| **Madhya** | Wine/Honey | Indulgence, charm, illusion; lively pleasure-seeking children; with spiritual inclination can become imaginative and thoughtful |
| **Shuddha Jala** | Pure Water | Clarity, innocence, adaptability; sensitive children with spiritual promise; ocean of potential — patience, healing, noble intentions |

### Example Calculation
- D1 Lagna = 6° Aquarius (odd sign)
- 6° falls in the **2nd division** (4°17" – 8°34")
- For odd signs, 2nd division = **Ksheera (Milk)**
- Result: Emotionally connected child; children uplift parents; wise, spiritually rich child

## 6. Key Rules for D7 Reading

1. **Always read D1 and D7 together** — the D7 does not stand alone
2. **5th house** in both D1 and D7 is the primary house of children
3. **Jupiter** is the natural karaka (significator) for children — its condition is critical
4. **D7 Lagna lord** strength determines overall progeny fortune
5. When reading for a couple, compare **both partners' D7 charts**
6. The **Sapt Ras of the D1 Lagna** reveals the karmic quality of the native's relationship with children
7. The **7th house in D7** (3rd from 5th) shows conception and relations with opposite sex
