## 8. Crystal Durability

- **Shelf life**: 2–3 years if not broken naturally; or cleanse/charge weekly/fortnightly indefinitely.
- **Hardness**: Higher Mohs rating (quartz, amethyst, sapphire) = more durable. Softer stones (selenite, malachite) chip or crack more easily.
- **Light/Heat**: Quartz-family stones (Amethyst, Rose Quartz, Citrine) fade in prolonged sunlight.
- **Environment**: Humidity affects Pyrite (colour change); water dissolves Selenite; chemicals can discolour others.

### When a Crystal Breaks
It is believed the crystal has fulfilled its intention. Bury the broken pieces in soil or submerge in flowing water with gratitude.
