# Chakra Healing Reference

## The 7 Chakras — Full Details

### 1. Root Chakra — Muladhara
- **Location**: Base of spine (tailbone back, pubic bone front)
- **Element**: Earth | **Color**: Red
- **Energy**: Survival, security, physical identity, safety, grounding, manifestation
- **Imbalance signs**: Fearful, anxious, insecure, frustrated
- **Health issues**: Obesity, anorexia nervosa, knee troubles, hip/leg/lower back problems
- **Crystals**: Red Jasper, Garnet, Smoky Quartz, Obsidian, Black Tourmaline

---

### 2. Sacral Chakra — Svadisthana
- **Location**: Below the navel, rooted into the spine
- **Element**: Water | **Color**: Orange
- **Energy**: Sexuality, creativity, intuition, confidence, self-worth, emotions, friendliness
- **Note**: Childhood emotional expression patterns impact this chakra
- **Imbalance signs**: Emotional instability, manipulative tendencies, obsessive thoughts, lethargy
- **Health issues**: Kidney weakness, stiff lower back, constipation, muscle spasms
- **Crystals**: Carnelian, Agate, Orange Calcite, Tiger Eye, Citrine, Moonstone

---

### 3. Solar Plexus Chakra — Manipura
- **Location**: Upper abdomen / stomach area
- **Element**: Fire | **Color**: Yellow
- **Energy**: Strength, passion, ego, impulses, anger, psychic development
- **Imbalance signs**: Under-confidence, confusion, people-pleasing, submissiveness, depression
- **Health issues**: Digestive difficulties, liver problems, diabetes, nervous exhaustion, food allergies
- **Crystals**: Citrine, Topaz, Yellow Calcite, Yellow Aventurine, Malachite

---

### 4. Heart Chakra — Anahata
- **Location**: Centre of chest
- **Element**: Air | **Color**: Green
- **Energy**: Love, compassion, emotional balance, forgiveness, spirituality; governs ability to love self and others
- **Imbalance signs**: Self-pity, paranoia, indecisiveness, fear of letting go, feeling unworthy of love, lack of empathy
- **Health issues**: Heart attack, blood pressure issues, insomnia, breathing difficulties
- **Crystals**: Rose Quartz, Kunzite, Watermelon Tourmaline, Jade, Green Calcite, Rhodonite

---

### 5. Throat Chakra — Vishuddi
- **Location**: Throat (lower neck, voice chords)
- **Element**: Ether | **Color**: Blue
- **Energy**: Creative expression, thought, speech, writing
- **Imbalance signs**: Holding back feelings, timidity, weakness, inexpressiveness
- **Health issues**: Hyper/hypo thyroid, skin irritations, ear infections, sore throat, inflammations, back pain
- **Crystals**: Aquamarine, Azurite, Lapis Lazuli, Turquoise

---

### 6. Third Eye Chakra — Ajna
- **Location**: Centre of forehead
- **Element**: Light | **Color**: Indigo
- **Energy**: Psychic ability, higher intuition, spirit and light energies; gateway for guidance and Higher Self
- **Imbalance signs**: Non-assertiveness, fear of success, OR opposite — egotism
- **Health issues**: Headaches, blurred vision, blindness, eyestrain
- **Crystals**: Amethyst, Sodalite, Lapis Lazuli, Purple Fluorite, Black Obsidian

---

### 7. Crown Chakra — Sahasrara
- **Location**: Behind the top of the skull
- **Element**: Consciousness | **Color**: Violet or White
- **Energy**: Spirituality, enlightenment, wisdom, cosmic consciousness
- **Imbalance signs**: Frustration, no spark of joy, destructive feelings
- **Health issues**: Migraines, frequent headaches, anxiety, depression
- **Crystals**: Clear Quartz Crystal, Oregon Opal, Diamond, Amethyst
