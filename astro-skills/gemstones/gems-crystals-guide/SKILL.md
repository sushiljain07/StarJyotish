## ROSE QUARTZ

**Colour:** Pink  
**Chakra:** Heart Chakra

### Healing Properties
- Aids chest and lung problems
- Strengthens heart and circulation
- Supports healing of Alzheimer's, Parkinson's, and dementia

### Recommended Position
Use as an accessory (jewellery) or place the stone in the bedroom.

### Attributes & Energy
Rose Quartz is the stone of unconditional love, emotional harmony, compassion, and self-worth.
It is particularly helpful during emotional challenges and for nurturing romantic relationships.

### Forms & Placement Guide

| Form | Purpose | Best Placement |
|------|---------|----------------|
| **Sphere** | Emotional harmony, gentle energy flow | Living rooms, meditation spaces, bedrooms |
| **Tumbled Stone** | Portable emotional support, self-love | Pocket or bag during stressful situations |
| **Tree** | Attracting love and harmony | Work desks, bedside tables |
| **Bracelet** | Daily emotional healing, self-love | Worn on wrist throughout the day |
| **Angels** | Divine love, emotional protection | Altar or bedside |
| **Tower** | Manifesting love, emotional healing | Work desk, altar, self-care area |
| **Cluster** | Amplifying love, cleansing emotional spaces | Family rooms or bedrooms |
| **Pendant** | Personal love, emotional resilience | Worn close to the heart |

## 7 CHAKRA

**Colour:** 7 colours (one per chakra)  
**Chakra:** All 7 chakras simultaneously

### Attributes & Energy
- Balances all energy centres
- Aligns and opens the chakras
- Promotes overall harmony
- Enhances positive energy flow
- Deepens and enhances meditation practice

### The 7 Chakras (Root → Crown)
1. **Root (Muladhara)** — Red — Grounding, safety
2. **Sacral (Svadhisthana)** — Orange — Creativity, emotion
3. **Solar Plexus (Manipura)** — Yellow — Confidence, willpower
4. **Heart (Anahata)** — Green/Pink — Love, compassion
5. **Throat (Vishuddha)** — Blue — Communication, truth
6. **Third Eye (Ajna)** — Indigo — Intuition, clarity
7. **Crown (Sahasrara)** — Violet/White — Spirituality, connection

### Common Forms
Bracelets, Stones, Pendants, Trees, Pyramids — all designed to represent all 7 chakras together.

### Best Use
- Meditation practice (hold or place on the body)
- Anywhere energy balancing is needed
- Daily wear as bracelet or pendant for ongoing alignment

## Response Guidelines

When answering crystal questions using this skill:
1. **Identify the need** — emotional, physical, spiritual, or spatial
2. **Match to crystal** — use the Quick Reference or detailed sections
3. **Recommend a form** — based on how they want to use it (wear, place, meditate)
4. **Give placement advice** — specific room or position for best effect
5. **Keep tone warm and practical** — blend the metaphysical with actionable guidance

Always note that crystal healing is a complementary wellness practice and not a substitute for medical treatment.
