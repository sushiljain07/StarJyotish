- Planetary positions and house placements
- Strength and weakness of each planet
- Benefic vs malefic planetary influences
- Conjunction and aspect patterns

✅ **Ascendant (Lagna) & Moon Sign**
- Moon sign determines emotional compatibility
- Ascendant determines overall suitability
- Must check planetary rulership of ascendant

✅ **Planetary Qualities**
- Is the planet exalted, debilitated, or neutral?
- Is it benefic or malefic for the ascendant?
- House placements and aspects
- Conjunction with other planets

✅ **Planetary Transits (Dashas)**
- Current mahadasha and antardasha
- Whether to strengthen or pacify the planet
- Timing of when benefits will manifest

✅ **House Considerations**
- I, V, VI, IX, X Houses (generally beneficial)
- VIII, XI, XII Houses (need careful analysis)
- House lord strength and placement

✅ **Personalized Problems or Goals**
- Specific life challenges needing relief
- Particular goals to be achieved
- Life areas requiring strengthening

✅ **Gemstone Suitability Check**
- Compatibility with birth chart
- Compatibility with planetary positions
- Mandatory trial period before permanent wear

   - Red for Ruby, White for Pearl, Red for Coral, Green for Emerald, Yellow for YS, etc.

   - How's your sleep quality?
   - Observe dreams - are they good or disturbing?
   - Any physical changes (good or bad)?

   - **Bad effects**: Do NOT recommend - stone is incompatible

1. Wrap stone in relevant colored cloth
2. Keep under pillow while sleeping for **3 consecutive nights**
3. Check sleep quality and dream quality
4. If sleep good and dreams positive: Compatible
5. If sleep disturbed and dreams negative: Not compatible

1. Sit in meditation posture with stone in palm
2. Close your eyes and meditate for 20-30 minutes
3. Keep gemstone in palm and check inner feeling
4. Repeat for **3 consecutive days**
5. If feel positive energy: Compatible
6. If feel negative or uncomfortable: Not compatible
