# Blue Sapphire (Neelam) — Saturn (Shani)

**Ruling planet:** Saturn (Shani). **Rashis:** Capricorn (Makar) and Aquarius (Kumbh).

Blue Sapphire is one of the **most powerful gemstones** in Vedic astrology — it can give dramatic positive *and* negative results quickly. A thorough chart check is essential, and a **mandatory 3-day trial** must be done before long-term wear.

## Hard prohibitions

- Reject stones with **black or red speckles**.
- Never wear with **Ruby, Red Coral, or Pearl**.

## Common myths — debunked

1. **"If kundali favours the stone, no trial is needed"** — False. Even a chart-favoured Neelam requires a 3-day trial.
2. **"Any stone can take your life from 0 to 100 or vice versa"** — Exaggerated. Be respectful of Neelam; it is powerful but not magical in either direction.
3. **"Keep it under the pillow for 3 nights to check for bad dreams"** — This is a valid trial method but not the *only* method; the arm-bind or meditation method work equally well.
4. **"Neelam causes cough, cold, accidents, or bad events"** — This is a **myth**. Neelam that suits the chart will not cause illness or misfortune.

## What it does (claimed effects)

- Brings discipline and structure to the native's life.
- Delivers **instant abundance** in wealth and career — when it suits the chart.
- Balances the throat chakra; strengthens communication.
- Protects from negative energies.
- Relieves anxiety, calms composure, and supports intuition.
- Benefits the nervous system and bones.
