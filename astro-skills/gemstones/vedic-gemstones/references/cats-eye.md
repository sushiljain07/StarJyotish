# Cat's Eye (Lehsunia) — Ketu

**Ruling planet:** Ketu (south lunar node).

Cat's Eye is associated with the moksha-oriented, detached, and sometimes sudden energy of Ketu. It can give **sudden results** — both positive and negative — so a 3-day compatibility trial is mandatory for everyone.

## Universal rule

**For every ascendant below**, Cat's Eye should be recommended **only during the dasha periods of Ketu**, and **strictly only after a 3-day trial** for suitability. There is no "lifetime wear" recommendation for Cat's Eye in this tradition.

## Donations for Ketu

On Tuesday or Saturday, give any one of: black/blue clothes, black til (sesame seeds), dog seva (feed and care for dogs), donations to a Bhairav mandir, or alcohol offered at such mandirs — to appropriate recipients (not family).
