# Diamond (Heera) — Venus

**Ruling planet:** Venus (Shukra). **Rashis:** Libra (Tula) and Taurus (Vrishabh).

Diamond is the stone of love, beauty, charm, marital harmony, and material wealth. It supports the reproductive system and urinary tract, and stimulates the crown chakra for spiritual advancement.

## Wearing details

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Metal | White Gold or Silver (preferred); Yellow Gold acceptable if Jupiter is strong in kundali or in its own rashi |
| Finger | Ring finger (Third finger) for **love**; Index finger for **luxury/wealth** |
| Day to start | Friday (Shukla Paksha preferred) |
| Beej Mantra | ॐ द्रां द्रीं द्रौं सः शुक्राय नमः (*Om dram dreem draum sa: shukraya namah*) |
| Age of stone | Almost a lifetime (a few special cases: 2 years) |
| Minimum size | 1.4 carats (solitaire) |
| Price range | ₹8 lakh–10 lakh+ per carat (extremely expensive) |
| Origin | India (famous in Surat), China |
| Colour | Transparent (colourless preferred) |

**Critical — non-touching stone:** Diamond is the **only gemstone that must NOT touch the wearer's skin**. The ring setting must hold the stone away from the skin. Diamond edges are extremely sharp.

**Lab-grown diamonds:** Avoid — they do not carry the same planetary energy as natural stones.

## Donations for Venus

On Friday, give any one of: dahi (curd), rice, kheer, milk, sugar, perfume, or wedding-related donation — to needy persons (not to family members).

## Who can wear it broadly

**Taurus, Gemini, Virgo, Libra, Capricorn, and Aquarius** ascendants. Combinations:

- Diamond + Emerald
- Diamond + Blue Sapphire
- Diamond + Emerald + Blue Sapphire

## Ascendant-by-ascendant guidance

- **Aries** — not auspicious. Venus is enemy of Mars and is house lord of II and VII. Best avoided. If it must be worn: only during Venus's dasha, and only with Venus in II, VII, or XII house.
- **Taurus** — highly favourable. Venus is house lord of I and VI. Venus in I, V, IX, X, or XI → benefited the most; wearable lifelong.
- **Gemini** — Venus is house lord of V and XII; XII placement of Venus is highly beneficial. Venus in I, V, VII, IX, or X → wearable.
- **Cancer** — Venus in IV, IX, or XI → can be recommended during Venus's dasha, only after a compatibility trial.
- **Leo** — not a good placement; creates obstacles. Best avoided. If it must be worn: Venus in IV, X, or XI with ongoing Venus dasha. **Take care: never wear ruby and diamond together.**
- **Virgo** — Venus is house lord of II and IX. Venus in II, V, VII, or IX → wearable lifelong.
- **Libra** — auspicious. Venus is the ascendant lord, house lord of I and VIII (long life). Venus in I, IV, V, VI, VIII, IX, or X → wearable.
- **Scorpio** — keep away. Venus is house lord of VII and XII. In special cases: only during Venus's dasha, only if Venus is in V, VII, or XII house. **Trial mandatory.**
- **Sagittarius** — not highly recommended. Venus is house lord of VI and XI. Wear if Venus is in II, IV, or XI house, during Venus's ongoing dasha.
- **Capricorn** — wearable for good gains. Venus is house lord of V and X. Venus in I, III, V, IX, X, or XI → wearable lifelong.
- **Aquarius** — potent here. Venus is house lord of IV and IX. Venus in I, II, IV, IX, or X → highly benefited.
- **Pisces** — best avoided (Venus inimical to Jupiter; house lord of III and VIII). If it must be worn: only during Venus's dasha, with Venus in I, III, or XI house. **Trial required.**

## Hard prohibitions

- Diamond should **never touch the skin**.
- Never recommend **Red Coral, Pearl, or Ruby** with diamond.
- A good-quality diamond must have **no coloured speckles** — definitely no red dots.

## What it does (claimed effects)

- Wearer admires love and beauty.
- Enhances physical appearance; brings charm.
- Strengthens marital relationships; brings love and happiness.
- Attracts wealth and financial opportunities.
- Supports reproductive system and urinary tract.
- Stimulates the **crown chakra** — spiritual advancement, enlightenment.

## Special note on males

**Opal / double fire opal / black opal is a better substitute for diamond, especially for males**, because diamond is said to make male natives lustful in nature.

## Substitutes (must be heavier than the recommended diamond)

Opal, White Sapphire, natural Zircons.
