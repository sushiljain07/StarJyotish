# Emerald (Panna) — Mercury

**Ruling planet:** Mercury (Budh). **Major rashis:** Gemini (Mithun) and Virgo (Kanya).

Emerald is the stone of intellect, communication, and commerce. It strengthens wisdom, rationality, oratorical skill, and brings opportunities for income and business. Opens the heart chakra.

## Wearing details

- **Metal:** Gold or silver.
- **Finger:** Little finger.
- **Day to start:** Shukla Paksha Wednesday.
- **Activation:** Beej mantra of Mercury — *Om bram breem braum sa: budhaya namah*.

## Who can wear it broadly

**Taurus, Gemini, Virgo, Libra, Capricorn, and Aquarius** ascendants can wear emerald, including in combinations:

- Emerald + Blue Sapphire
- Emerald + Diamond
- Emerald + Blue Sapphire + Diamond

## Ascendant-by-ascendant guidance

- **Aries** — not a good stone (Mars not compatible with Mercury). Recommend only during Mercury major or sub-dashas, and only if Mercury is in III, VII, or X. If Mercury is in VI, can be worn during Mercury's dasha for someone needing to win **legal battles** or facing too much workplace **competition** — stop once the purpose is served.
- **Taurus** — favourable (Mercury friendly with Venus). Mercury in I, II, V, IX, or X → wearable lifelong.
- **Gemini** — most suitable. Mercury is house lord of I and IV. Mercury in I, IV, V, IX, or XI → wearable lifelong. If Mercury is in VIII or combust → 3-day compatibility trial first.
- **Cancer** — not ideal (Mercury is house lord of III and XII; no major benefits). If considered, only during Mercury's mahadasha and only if Mercury is in III, IV, VII, or XI.
- **Leo** — wealth-generating stone. Wear when Mercury is in II, IV, V, VII, IX, X, or XI, especially during Mercury's major and sub periods.
- **Virgo** — auspicious (Mercury is house lord of I and X). Mercury in I, II, IV, V, IX, X, or XI → wearable lifelong.
- **Libra** — good placement (Mercury is friendly with Venus, and is house lord of IX and XII). Mercury in I, IV, V, VII, IX, X, or XI → wearable lifelong. **Special caveat:** if a Libra native with XII-house Mercury is involved in import/export or other foreign-contact business, do a trial first.
- **Scorpio** — not normally recommended (Mercury enemy of ascendant lord Mars). After a trial, can be worn by those with Mercury in IV, VII, or XI house, only during Mercury's dashas.
- **Sagittarius** — wearable (Jupiter is ascendant lord; Mercury is house lord of VII and X). During Mercury dashas, those with Mercury in I, VII, IX, X, or XI should wear it.
- **Capricorn** — Mercury is house lord of VI and IX. Mercury in I, II, V, IX, or X → wearable lifelong. Ensures luck and supports overcoming challenges.
- **Aquarius** — Mercury is house lord of V and VIII. Mercury in I, IV, V, or IX → wearable lifelong, favorable.
- **Pisces** — (not explicitly listed for emerald in the source; emerald is not among the favorable stones for Pisces, which is Yellow-Sapphire/Coral/Pearl-friendly).

## Hard prohibitions

- Never recommend wearing **Red Coral, Pearl, or Ruby** along with emerald.

## What it does (claimed effects)

- Success and fame.
- Wards off evil energies.
- Enhances wisdom, rationality, communication, intelligence.
- Creativity, sharp-mindedness, oratorical skill.
- Attracts new opportunities for income and business.
- Removes stress, anxiety, depression.
- Cures eye, skin, nerve, throat, liver, kidney issues; helps with sore throat, kidney stones, diabetes.
- Opens the **heart chakra** — sensitivity to love and compassion; blesses with blissful marriage and good children.

## Quality and sourcing

Good quality emeralds come from **Colombia, Russia, and Brazil.**

## Substitutes (must be heavier than the recommended emerald)

Green Tourmaline, Green Aventurine, Green Onyx, Peridot, Green Jade, Green Agate.
