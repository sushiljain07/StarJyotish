# Hessonite (Gomed) — Rahu

**Ruling planet:** Rahu (north lunar node).

Hessonite addresses the karmic, illusion-laden energy of Rahu. It protects from negative energies, black magic, and evil spirits, supports business and career gains during Rahu periods, and stimulates the third-eye chakra for the spiritual journey.

## Donations for Rahu

On Tuesday or Saturday, give any one of: water, sharbat, blue-coloured clothes, black-coloured things, hair donation, medicines, or donations to blind hospitals/institutions for physically challenged persons — to the needy (not family).

## Hard prohibitions

- Never wear Gomed with **Red Coral, Pearl, Yellow Sapphire, or Ruby.**
- **Stone selection:** stay clear of hessonites with **black dots** (cause problems in married life) or **two distinctive colours** in one stone (cause health troubles).

## What it does (claimed effects)

- Protects from negative energies, black magic, evil spirits.
- Promotes spiritual growth and deeper meditation.
- Improves digestive system and nervous system.
- Sudden gains in business and career.
- Stimulates the **third-eye chakra** — supports the spiritual journey.
