# Opal — Venus (Substitute for Diamond/Heera)

**Ruling planet:** Venus (Shukra). Opal is the **primary substitute** for Diamond/Heera.

## Wearing details

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Metal | Silver, White Gold, or Platinum |
| Finger | **Left hand** (being a substitute); index finger for luxury/wealth; ring finger for love |
| Skin contact | Yes — skin-touching stone (unlike the primary Diamond which must not touch skin) |
| Day to start | Friday (Shukla Paksha preferred) |
| Beej Mantra | Same as Venus/Diamond: ॐ द्रां द्रीं द्रौं सः शुक्राय नमः (*Om dram dreem draum sa: shukraya namah*) |
| Price | Affordable (price increases with colour variation) |
| Origin | Australia (primary) |
| Colour | White, black, and many others; Fire Opal is a notable variety |

## Size formula

**Opal size = (body weight in kg ÷ 10) + 3 carats**

Example: A person weighing 70 kg should wear a 7 + 3 = **10 carat** opal.

## Who can wear it

Anyone for whom Diamond is recommended but budget is a constraint, or where Diamond is not suitable for the specific chart. Particularly noted as a **better option for male natives** (Diamond can make male wearers excessively indulgent in pleasure; Opal does not carry this risk).

## Notes

- Wearing substitutes on the **left hand or non-working hand** is acceptable and standard.
- Opal should be **skin-touching**, unlike Diamond.
- Avoid lab-grown or synthetic opals — use natural stones only.

## Other Venus substitutes

White Sapphire, Natural Zircons. (All substitutes must weigh more than the Diamond would have been prescribed at.)
