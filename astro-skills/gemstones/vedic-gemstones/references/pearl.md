# Pearl (Moti) — Moon

**Ruling planet:** Moon (Chandra). **Rashi:** Cancer (Kark). **Also known as:** Tarak Ratna, Chandra Ratna, Mukta.

Pearl is calming, soothing, and nurturing. It pacifies the Moon, brings emotional stability, peace of mind, and supports intuition, creativity, and spiritual practice.

## Wearing details

- **Metal:** Silver.
- **Finger:** Little finger.
- **Day to start:** Shukla Paksha Monday.
- **Activation:** Beej mantra of Moon — *Om shram shreem shraum sa: chandramase namah*.
- **Source:** Must be naturally sourced. Lab-grown pearls are useless as astrological remedies.

## Who is it most favorable for

Most auspicious for **Pisces, Scorpio, and Cancer** ascendants. They can wear classical combinations:

- Pearl + Yellow Sapphire
- Pearl + Coral
- Pearl + Yellow Sapphire + Coral

## Ascendant-by-ascendant guidance

- **Aries** — favourable; Moon is house lord of the IV house. Good if Moon is in lagna, II, IV, V, or IX.
- **Taurus** — totally avoid. Exception: Moon in lagna, OR during Moon mahadasha.
- **Gemini** — wearable if Moon is in II house, OR in X house in Pisces, during Moon mahadasha. **Always trial first.**
- **Cancer** — highly auspicious. Moon in I, VII, IX, X, or XI → great results. **But avoid if Moon is in V house in Scorpio.**
- **Leo** — generally not suitable. Exception: an exalted Moon in the X house.
- **Virgo** — wearable during Moon's mahadasha if Moon is in IX or XI house.
- **Libra** — advisable only during Moon's mahadasha if Moon is in III, IV, V, VII, IX, X, or XI house.
- **Scorpio** — Moon is house lord of the IX (divine blessings). Moon in II, V, VII, or IX → wearable lifelong, highly successful results.
- **Sagittarius** — totally avoid. Moon is house lord of the VIII (sudden loss, mishaps). Exception: if Jupiter, Sun, and Mars are all ill-placed and the native cannot wear those stones, pearl can be worn during Moon's mahadasha *and only if Moon is in the IX house*.
- **Capricorn** — only during Moon's mahadasha, only if Moon is placed in the V house. If Moon is in VII, pearl can be worn briefly to break a delay in marriage.
- **Aquarius** — totally avoid.
- **Pisces** — auspicious especially if Moon is in II, III, IV, V, or X house.

## Hard prohibitions

- Never wear pearl with **Blue Sapphire, Diamond, or Hessonite**.

## What it does (claimed effects)

- Pacifies the Moon and amplifies its powers in the wearer.
- Emotional stability, peace of mind, calmness, well-being.
- Improves intuition, insightfulness, creativity.
- Supports stomach, digestive system, and breast health.
- Enhances fertility; protects expecting mothers and unborn children; brings the feeling of motherhood in women.
- Aids meditation and spiritual practice.
- Promotes compassion and understanding between partners.
- Helps control anger.
- Opens the **sacral chakra** — improves creativity, intimacy, sensuality.

## Substitute (must be heavier than the recommended pearl)

Moonstone.
