# Red Coral (Moonga) — Mars

**Ruling planet:** Mars (Mangal).

Red Coral is the stone of energy, courage, strength, and physical stamina. It acts as a shield against accidents, injuries, and enemies, and is often suggested to reduce the malefic effects of Mars.

## Wearing details

- **Metal:** Copper.
- **Finger:** Ring finger.
- **Day to start:** Shukla Paksha Tuesday.
- **Activation:** Beej mantra of Mars — *Om kram kreem kraum sa: bhaumaya namah*.

## Who is it most favorable for

Most beneficial for **Aries, Cancer, Leo, Sagittarius, and Pisces** ascendants.

## Ascendant-by-ascendant guidance

- **Aries** — Mars is lord of the I and VIII house. Wearable if Mars is in I, III, V, VI, IX, X, or XI. Combinations: coral + yellow sapphire + ruby, coral + ruby, or coral + yellow sapphire.
- **Taurus** — Venus rules; avoid Mars stones generally. Exception: Mars in IV, IX, or XI house, *only* during Mars mahadasha and after a proper trial.
- **Gemini** — totally avoided. Mercury rules; Mars becomes problematic.
- **Cancer** — highly beneficial. Mars is house lord of V and X. Mars in II, III, V, VII, or X → wearable lifelong. Combos: pearl + coral + yellow sapphire, pearl + coral, or coral + yellow sapphire.
- **Leo** — Mars is house lord of IV and IX. Mars in I, III, IV, VI, IX, X, or XI → wearable lifelong, doubles fortune. Combos: ruby + coral, yellow sapphire + coral, or ruby + yellow sapphire + coral.
- **Virgo** — Mercury rules; Mars problematic. Avoid.
- **Libra** — Venus rules; Mars problematic. Avoid.
- **Scorpio** — Mars is auspicious; house lord of I and VI. Mars in I, III, V, VI, X, or XI → wearable confidently. Combos: yellow sapphire + coral, pearl + coral, or all three.
- **Sagittarius** — Mars is auspicious (ascendant lord is Jupiter). Mars in I, V, IX, or X → ideal candidates. Blesses with intellectual capabilities.
- **Capricorn** — avoid at most. If no other stones are suitable, can be worn during Mars dashas and only if Mars is in I, IV, X, or XI. **Trial period mandatory.**
- **Aquarius** — avoid. If no other stones are suitable, can be worn during Mars dashas and only if Mars is in III, V, IX, X, or XI. **Trial period mandatory.**
- **Pisces** — favorable (ascendant lord is Jupiter; Mars is house lord of II and IX). Mars in I, II, III, VI, IX, X, or XI → highly positive. Combos: coral + yellow sapphire, coral + pearl, or all three.

## Hard prohibitions

- **Should NOT be worn by Mangaliks** (those with Mars dosha).
- Never recommend with **Diamond, Blue Sapphire, or Emerald**.

## What it does (claimed effects)

- Strong, vigorous, protective energy.
- Develops assertiveness, overcomes fears.
- Imparts energy, strength, passion.
- Promotes physical strength and stamina.
- Shield against accidents, injuries, enemies.
- Improves blood circulation and heart health.
- Enhances ambition and determination.
- Helps overcome delays in endeavours.
- Reduces the malefic effects of Mars.

## Quality and sourcing

**Italian and Japanese** red coral are the highest quality.

## Substitutes (must be heavier than the recommended coral)

Carnelian, Red Onyx, Red Agate.
