# Ruby (Manikya) — Sun

**Ruling planet:** Sun (Surya). **Rashi:** Leo (Singh). **Also known as:** Padmaraga, Ratnaraj, Ratnanayaka.

Ruby is a remedy for an afflicted Sun in the natal chart. It absorbs and amplifies solar energy — leadership, authority, vitality, creativity, fame, power, materialistic wealth.

## Wearing details

- **Metal:** Copper.
- **Finger:** Ring finger.
- **Day to start:** Shukla Paksha Sunday (waxing-moon Sunday).
- **Activation:** See `procedure-and-mantras.md`. Beej mantra is the Sun's: *Om hram hreem hraum sa: suryaya namah*.

## Who is it most favorable for

Most favorable for natives with **Leo, Aries, or Sagittarius** as their ascendant. These three lagnas can also wear classical combinations:

- Ruby + Yellow Sapphire
- Ruby + Coral
- Ruby + Yellow Sapphire + Coral

## Ascendant-by-ascendant guidance

- **Aries** — favourable.
- **Taurus** — avoid. The Sun is enemy of the ascendant lord Venus; even an exalted Sun in the XII house won't yield favorable results.
- **Gemini** — only if Sun is placed in the III or XI house, OR during Sun's mahadasha/antardasha. Also weigh Sun-Mercury conjunction: if those two are conjoined in the III, IV, or XI house, recommend Ruby + Emerald together.
- **Cancer** — strong yes if Sun is in the X house (excellent for wealth). The IX or V house Sun is also good.
- **Leo** — recommend when Sun is in the lagna, V, IX, or XI house. Wearable lifelong, excellent results.
- **Virgo** — a big NO.
- **Libra** — only after a thorough chart check; advisable during Sun's mahadasha, only if Saturn or Venus aren't well placed, or if Sun is in II, VII, or XI house.
- **Scorpio** — extremely auspicious if Sun is in the V, VI, IX, or X house.
- **Sagittarius** — Sun in I, V, or IX → wearable for life. Sun in II, III, IV, or VII → only during Sun's dasha. Sun in VI, VIII, or XI → refrain from wearing.
- **Capricorn** — not recommended at all (Sun is enemy of the ascendant lord Saturn).
- **Aquarius** — first check Venus's position; consider only if Sun is in the III, VII, X, or XI house.
- **Pisces** — usually not recommended; can be considered if Sun is in the II or VI house.

## Hard prohibitions

- Do NOT recommend if Sun sits with Rahu, Ketu, or Saturn in the same house.
- **Pisces, Virgo, Capricorn, Libra, Gemini, and Aquarius** ascendants — generally not recommended.
- A weak Sun in the horoscope → do not wear ruby.
- Anyone in Saturn-related businesses → avoid.
- Never wear ruby with Blue Sapphire or Hessonite.

## What it does (claimed effects)

- Confidence, courage, protection from enemies.
- Fame, power, luck, materialistic wealth — when Sun is well-placed.
- Strengthens love and passion in partnerships.
- Grounds and balances the mind.
- Energises the **root chakra** — feelings of security, stability, safety.
- Improves heart health and blood circulation.

## Quality and sourcing

- Highest quality is described as **"pigeon blood"** — deep, rich red.
- Pure natural sources: **Burma, Madagascar, Thailand, Sri Lanka.**

## Substitutes (must be heavier in carat than the recommended ruby)

Red tourmaline, red garnet, red spinel, red beryl, red topaz.
