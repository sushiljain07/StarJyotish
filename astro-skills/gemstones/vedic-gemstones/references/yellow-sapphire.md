# Yellow Sapphire (Pukhraj) — Jupiter

**Ruling planet:** Jupiter (Guru). **Also known as:** Pushraja, Peetmani.

Yellow Sapphire (often abbreviated YS) is one of the most universally beneficial astrological stones. It supports marriage (especially for women), career, studies, health (digestive, liver, kidney), and is calming and grace-giving.

## Wearing details

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Metal | Gold |
| Finger | Index finger (First finger) |
| Day to start | Thursday (Shukla Paksha preferred) |
| Beej Mantra | ॐ ग्रां ग्रीं ग्रौं सः गुरवे नमः (*Om gram greem graum sa: guruve namah*) |
| Age of stone | 3–3.5 years (replace after) |
| Price range | ₹8,000–50,000+ per carat (very expensive) |
| Origin | Sri Lanka (primary), Tanzania, Madagascar, Burma |
| Colour preference | Yellow — darker shade is preferable |

**Primary purpose:** Success and fame. For a Jupiter + Venus combined remedy, consider donating toward a wedding or sponsoring an unmarried person's marriage expenses.

## Donations for Jupiter

On Thursday, give any one of: chana dal, haldi (turmeric), yellow clothes, arhar dal, brass (petal) utensils, yellow fruits — typically offered to a temple priest (mandir poojari).

## Ascendant-by-ascendant guidance

- **Aries** — Jupiter is house lord of IX and XII, and friend of ascendant lord Mars. Jupiter in I, IV, V, or IX → wearable lifelong. During Jupiter's dasha, most natives can wear YS *except* when Jupiter is in X (debilitated).
- **Taurus** — Jupiter inimical to Venus; not many benefits. Can still be worn by those with Jupiter in III, IV, or XI house during Jupiter's dashas. **They must not wear diamond or emerald with it.**
- **Gemini** — Jupiter loses potency with Mercury-ruled lagna. House lord of VII and X. No major benefits, but in adverse situations YS can be recommended when Jupiter is in II, VII, or X house, during Jupiter dashas.
- **Cancer** — strong placement. Jupiter is house lord of VI and IX. Jupiter in I, II, V, IX, X, or XI → wearable lifelong, excellent opportunities. Combos: pearl + YS, or coral + YS, or all three.
- **Leo** — auspicious. House lord of V and VIII. Jupiter in I, IV, V, or IX → wearable lifelong. Jupiter in X or XI → during Jupiter dashas. Combos: YS + coral, YS + ruby, or all three.
- **Virgo** — no major benefits, but if YS must be worn, do so during Jupiter's dashas with Jupiter in IV, VII, or XI house.
- **Libra** — Jupiter is enemy of ascendant lord Venus and a problem creator unless well-placed. House lord of III and VI. Wear only during Jupiter dashas, only if Jupiter is in III, VI, IX, X, or XI house. **Do not wear diamond with YS in this case.**
- **Scorpio** — good. Jupiter is house lord of II and V. Jupiter in I, II, V, IX, or X → wearable lifelong. Jupiter in IV, VII, or XI → during Jupiter's dasha only. Combos: YS + coral, YS + pearl, or all three.
- **Sagittarius** — excellent. Jupiter is the ascendant lord and house lord of I and IV. Jupiter in I, IV, V, or IX → wearable lifelong. Jupiter in X or XI → during Jupiter's favourable dashas. Combos: YS + coral, YS + ruby, or all three.
- **Capricorn** — Jupiter is house lord of III and XII. Wear only during Jupiter dashas, and only if Jupiter is in III, IV, VII, or XI house.
- **Aquarius** — Jupiter is house lord of II and XI. Jupiter in II, X, or XI → consider during Jupiter dashas.
- **Pisces** — excellent. Jupiter is the ascendant lord and house lord of X. Jupiter in I, V, IX, or X → wearable lifelong. Jupiter in II, III, IV, or VII → during Jupiter's dashas only.

## Hard prohibitions

- Never recommend **diamond, blue sapphire, hessonite, or emerald** alongside YS.
- For natives with **Jupiter in I house** specifically, it's best NOT to wear YS — it makes them excessively devoted to society at their own cost.

## What it does (claimed effects)

- Brings a graceful demeanour.
- A good married life; helps make right decisions at the right time.
- Success in studies and career; improves social and financial status.
- Boon to digestive health; supports liver and kidney.
- Excellent stone for **females** — bestows a loving, prosperous groom, marital bliss, good progeny.
- Reduces anger; keeps wearer calm and composed.
- Heals kidney, mouth, skin, throat, rheumatism, fever, cough.
- Helps cut down body fat.
- Activates the **solar plexus chakra** — boosts self-confidence.

## Quality and sourcing

Best quality available in **Sri Lanka**; also procured from **Tanzania, Madagascar, Burma**.
