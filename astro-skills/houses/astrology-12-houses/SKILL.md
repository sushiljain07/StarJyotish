# Astrology Houses: 12 Houses & Planetary Influences

Comprehensive guide to understanding the 12 astrological houses, planetary effects, and their combinations in Vedic astrology.

## Overview

The 12 houses in astrology represent different domains of human life. When planets occupy specific houses, they modify and enhance the house's natural characteristics. This skill provides detailed interpretations of:

- Individual house meanings and attributes
- How planets affect each house
- House combinations and their significance
- Practical applications for birth chart interpretation
- Hindi translations for all concepts

---

## 1. First House (Ascendant / Lagna)

### Core Attributes
**Self, personality, physical appearance, vitality, beginnings, demeanor, outlook on life**

The First House represents how you approach life and project yourself to the world. It's the foundation of your entire birth chart.

### What It Signifies
- Your physical body and appearance
- Your personality and temperament
- Your natural inclinations and talents
- How others perceive you initially
- New beginnings and fresh starts
- Self-confidence and self-expression

### Planetary Effects on First House

- **Sun in 1st**: Strong personality, leadership qualities, confidence, authority
- **Moon in 1st**: Emotional sensitivity, nurturing nature, changing moods, adaptability
- **Mars in 1st**: Courage, aggression, assertiveness, competitive spirit
- **Mercury in 1st**: Communication skills, intellect, curiosity, versatility
- **Jupiter in 1st**: Optimism, wisdom, generosity, expansive nature
- **Venus in 1st**: Beauty, charm, artistic nature, social grace
- **Saturn in 1st**: Discipline, responsibility, seriousness, longevity
- **Rahu in 1st**: Unconventional appearance, ambitious nature, worldly interests
- **Ketu in 1st**: Spiritual inclinations, detached nature, mysterious aura

### House Combinations with 1st House
- **1st + 7th Houses**: Strong marriage focus; personal growth through relationships
- **1st + 10th Houses**: Ambitious, career-focused personality; public recognition

### Hindi Translation
**पहला घर (लग्न स्थान)**
- **गुण**: खुद, व्यक्तित्व, शारीरिक रूप, शरुआत, आचरण, जीवन दृष्टिकोण
- **अर्थ**: यह आपके जीवन के प्रति दृष्टिकोण और विश्व में स्वयं को प्रस्तुत करने का तरीका दर्शाता है।

## Practical Application Tips

1. **House Strength Assessment**: Check if house lord is well-placed in the chart
2. **Planetary Aspects**: Notice which planets aspect each house from their positions
3. **Dashas & Transits**: House effects activate during planetary dasha and transits
4. **House Cusps**: Calculate precise house cusps using birth time (essential for accuracy)
5. **Conjunction vs Aspect**: Planets in a house have stronger effect than aspects to that house
