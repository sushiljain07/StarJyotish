# Basics — Houses, Signs, Lords, and Degrees

This file holds the foundational knowledge any marriage analysis depends on. Most users won't ask about it directly, but you'll need to reference it constantly inside the analysis.

## The 12 Houses (Bhavas)

A kundli has 12 houses. The 1st house is the Lagna (Ascendant) — the rising sign at the moment of birth. The houses count counterclockwise from there.

| # | House | Significations |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Lagna (Tanu) | Body, physique, self, personality, head, overall life |
| 2 | Dhana | Money, family, eating habits, speech, savings, face |
| 3 | Sahaja | Hard work, courage, younger siblings, short trips, communication, hands |
| 4 | Sukha | Mother, house, vehicles, property, comforts, heart, education (basic) |
| 5 | Putra | Education (higher), love life, children, creativity, romance, mantras, speculation |
| 6 | Ari | Diseases, debt, enemies, job, servants, pets, daily routine, litigation |
| **7** | **Yuvati / Kalatra** | **Spouse, marriage, partnership, business partners, public dealings, contracts** |
| 8 | Ayu / Randhra | Occult, longevity, in-laws, sudden events, transformation, inheritance, scandals |
| 9 | Bhagya | Father, guru, luck, religion, dharma, long journeys, higher learning, fortune |
| 10 | Karma | Career, profession, social status, fame, action in the world |
| 11 | Labha | Gains, friends, elder siblings, fulfillment of desires, networks, income |
| 12 | Vyaya | Foreign settlement, expenses, spirituality, bed pleasures, losses, isolation |

## Houses most relevant for marriage

- **7th (primary)** — spouse, marriage, partnership.
- **2nd (secondary)** — family expansion through marriage; the 2nd lord and 2nd-house transit triggers also bring marriage.
- **5th** — romance and love. Strong 5th-7th connection often signals love marriage.
- **8th** — the Mangalya sthana in some schools; longevity of the spouse and marital intimacy. Also in-laws.
- **11th** — fulfillment of desire (and a desire for marriage is a desire). 11th-house Jupiter transits sometimes trigger marriage in rare cases.
- **12th** — bed pleasures and foreign-spouse indications. Venus in the 12th is not bad for marriage; it's actually classically considered exalted in pleasure.

## The 12 Rashis (Signs) and their Lords

| # | Rashi (Sanskrit) | English | Lord (Planet) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Mesh | Aries | Mars (Mangal) |
| 2 | Vrishabh | Taurus | Venus (Shukra) |
| 3 | Mithun | Gemini | Mercury (Buddha) |
| 4 | Karka | Cancer | Moon (Chandra) |
| 5 | Singh | Leo | Sun (Surya) |
| 6 | Kanya | Virgo | Mercury (Budh) |
| 7 | Tula | Libra | Venus (Shukra) |
| 8 | Vrishchik | Scorpio | Mars (Mangal) |
| 9 | Dhanu | Sagittarius | Jupiter (Guru) |
| 10 | Makar | Capricorn | Saturn (Shani) |
| 11 | Kumbh | Aquarius | Saturn (Shani) |
| 12 | Meena | Pisces | Jupiter (Guru) |

This mapping is essential — to find the "lord of the 7th house", first identify which sign falls in the 7th house, then read off its lord from this table. Example: a Leo lagna has Aquarius (sign 11) in the 7th house; the 7th lord is Saturn.

## Attributes (traits) of each Rashi

These traits transfer to the spouse when read from the 7th house:

| Sign | Traits |
|---|---|
| Aries (Mesh) | New beginnings, energetic, assertive, adventurous, impulsive, pioneering |
| Taurus (Vrishabh) | Practical, reliable, sensual, materialistic, persistent |
| Gemini (Mithun) | Curious, communicative, adaptable, witty, intellectual |
| Cancer (Karka) | Emotional, nurturing, intuitive, sensitive, protective |
| Leo (Singh) | Confident, creative, bold, charismatic, ambitious, authoritative |
| Virgo (Kanya) | Analytical, critical, meticulous, practical, modest, perfectionist |
| Libra (Tula) | Diplomatic, balanced, aesthetic, social, cooperative |
| Scorpio (Vrishchik) | Intense, secretive, transformative, passionate, resilient |
| Sagittarius (Dhanu) | Optimistic, philosophical, adventurous, intellectual, independent |
| Capricorn (Makar) | Disciplined, ambitious, patient, practical, responsible (sometimes pessimistic) |
| Aquarius (Kumbh) | Innovative, humanitarian, eccentric, intellectual, detached |
| Pisces (Meena) | Compassionate, intuitive, artistic, dreamy, spiritual |

When the 7th house sign is Libra, the spouse tends to be diplomatic and aesthetic; when it's Capricorn, the spouse tends to be older or more disciplined; and so on.

## Degrees of planets (longitudes)

Each rashi spans 30°, so the full zodiac is 360°. The longitude of a planet in a chart is *(rashi number − 1) × 30 + degree-within-the-sign*.

| Rashi | Longitude range |
|---|---|
| Aries | 0°–30° |
| Taurus | 30°–60° |
| Gemini | 60°–90° |
| Cancer | 90°–120° |
| Leo | 120°–150° |
| Virgo | 150°–180° |
| Libra | 180°–210° |
| Scorpio | 210°–240° |
| Sagittarius | 240°–270° |
| Capricorn | 270°–300° |
| Aquarius | 300°–330° |
| Pisces | 330°–360° |

This longitude table is **essential for the Group B timing techniques** (Techniques 8–11) which add planetary longitudes and find the resulting rashi by mapping the sum back into the table.

**Worked example:** If Jupiter is at 25° in Sagittarius, its longitude is 240 + 25 = 265°. If Mercury is at 6° in Sagittarius, its longitude is 240 + 6 = 246°. The two added together = 511°. Since the sum exceeds 360°, subtract 360° → 151°, which falls in Virgo (150°–180°). So the resultant rashi is Virgo. Techniques 8 onward use this style of calculation extensively.

## Reading degrees from a chart

When a chart shows planets with degree notation like:
- Asc 26.65° Virgo
- Sun 19.48° Sagittarius
- Moon 8.20° Aquarius
- Mars 6.75° Cancer
- Mercury 29.38° Scorpio
- Jupiter 18.71° Taurus
- Venus 6.51° Aquarius
- Saturn 20.54° Aquarius
- Rahu 7.16° Pisces
- Ketu 7.16° Virgo

…you can immediately compute the Darakaraka by finding the lowest-degree planet (excluding Rahu and Ketu). In this example, Venus at 6.51° is the lowest, so **Venus is the Darakaraka**. (See `darakaraka.md` for what that means.)

## Aspects (Drishti) — what each planet aspects

Every planet aspects the 7th house from itself. Three planets — Mars, Jupiter, Saturn — have additional special aspects you must remember for marriage timing:

| Planet | Aspects (houses from itself) |
|---|---|
| All planets | 7th |
| **Mars** | 4th, 7th, 8th |
| **Jupiter** | 5th, 7th, 9th |
| **Saturn** | 3rd, 7th, 10th |

**Why this matters for marriage:** Many techniques fire when *Jupiter aspects* the 7th house, the 7th lord, the lagna lord, Venus, or the Darakaraka — and Jupiter's 5th and 9th aspects are doing the heavy lifting. Saturn's 3rd and 10th aspects do similar work for the "Saturn cements the union" rule (Technique 21).
