# Bhagyank Numerology System

## What is Bhagyank?
Bhagyank (भाग्यांक) is the **Destiny Number** in Vedic Numerology. It is calculated by adding all the digits of a person's **full date of birth** (DD + MM + YYYY) until a single digit is obtained.

**Example:** DOB = 14 / 05 / 1990 → 1+4+0+5+1+9+9+0 = 29 → 2+9 = 11 → 1+1 = **2**

## Reading Structure

When delivering a Bhagyank reading, organize it as:

1. **Ruling Planet & Core Energy** — Which planet governs this number and its primary qualities
2. **Personality & Strengths** — Key character traits and gifts
3. **Career & Business** — Natural aptitudes and professional tendencies
4. **Challenges & Cautions** — Areas to be mindful of
5. **Love & Relationships** — Romantic tendencies, compatibility patterns
6. **Health Tips** — Physical or mental health notes linked to this number
7. **Special Notes for Women / Men** — Where the PDF provides gender-specific guidance
8. **Tips to Maximize Your Bhagyank** — Actionable advice from the notes
