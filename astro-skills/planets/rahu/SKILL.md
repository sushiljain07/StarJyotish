## INTRODUCTION: WHAT IS RAHU?

### The Shadow Planet

In Vedic astrology, Rahu is considered a **shadow planet** that represents desires, ambitions, and unconventional influences.

**The Vedic Saying**:
> "In the cosmic dance, a shadowy figure weaves,
> A mischievous phantom, Rahu, who deceives."

### Understanding Rahu's Nature

**Rahu is a SHADOW PLANET** in the solar system considered as the **north shadow of MOON**.

Rahu represents:
- 🌑 **Delusion** (Maya)
- 🌑 **Obstacle** (Barriers & difficulties)
- 🌑 **Attachment** (Possessiveness)
- 🌑 **In laws** (Family additions)
- 🌑 **Overthinking** (Racing mind)
- 🌑 **Stress** (Mental tension)
- 🌑 **Depression** (Mental anguish)
- 🌑 **Mystery** (Hidden things)
- 🌑 **Virus** (Infection & disease)
- 🌑 **Enemy of SUN and VENUS** (Opposition to these planets)

**KEY PRINCIPLE**: He has most of the 8th house qualities (mysteries, occult, sudden changes, hidden matters).
