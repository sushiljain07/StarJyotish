## INTRODUCTION: WHAT IS SATURN?

### The Karmic Teacher

In Vedic astrology, Saturn is also known as **Shani** and is considered a **malefic planet**. When Shani comes into picture, it says:

**"There is no luck factor involved and only hard work counts for getting good results."**

**The Vedic Saying**:
> "Shani, the lord of karmic sway,
> Teaches us to persevere, come what may.
> Embrace the challenge, welcome the test,
> In the crucible of Saturn, we find our best.
> Though his trials may seem severe,
> In their midst, our strength does appear."

### Saturn's Divine Purpose

Saturn represents **karma in action** - the principle that we reap what we sow. It teaches discipline, responsibility, and wisdom through challenges and delays.
