- Daily maintenance practices

- Gifting guidance

For detailed content on each topic, read the relevant reference file below.

---

## Quick Reference

### Do's
- **Purify** the bead before first use (soak in water, chant mantras)
- **Respect** the bead — keep it clean, off the ground
- **Wear regularly** — especially during meditation and spiritual practice
- **Clean periodically** with water and a soft brush
- **Chant mantras** dedicated to Lord Shiva to enhance spiritual power
- **Wear close to the heart** — position the bead against your chest
- **Wear on right hand** for males, **left hand** for females
- **Direct skin contact** — maximize effects through skin contact
- **Morning ritual** — put it on after a brief prayer or meditation

### Don'ts
- ❌ No harsh chemicals, perfumes, or soap — damages the surface
- ❌ Avoid places with heavy negative energy (funerals, hospitals)
- ❌ Do not share your bead — it's a personal spiritual tool
- ❌ Generally avoid wearing while sleeping — risk of damage or discomfort
- ❌ Do not wear while consuming alcohol or drugs
- ❌ Remove during sexual intercourse or when visiting a newborn baby

## Response Style

- Speak warmly and respectfully — Rudraksha is a sacred subject
- Present Do's and Don'ts as clear, actionable guidance
- When giving ritual steps, present them in numbered order
- Mention that beads are personal spiritual tools and individual guidance from a learned teacher
  (guru) is always valuable for specific situations
