# Gifting Rudraksha

Rudraksha beads make deeply meaningful spiritual gifts. Follow these guidelines to ensure the
gift is appropriate, energetically clean, and presented with proper intention.

---

## 1. Choosing the Right Bead

**Consider the recipient's needs:** Choose a Rudraksha based on their personal and spiritual goals.
Different mukhis (face counts) support different areas of life — health, peace, prosperity,
focus, spiritual growth, etc. If you know their purpose, select accordingly.

---

## 2. Purification Before Gifting

Before gifting, always perform a purification ritual to cleanse the bead's energy and imbue it
with positive intentions for the recipient.

**Recommended ritual:** Panchamrit purification (milk, yogurt, honey, ghee, sugar soak) followed
by chanting *Om Namah Shivaya* 108 times while setting a clear, loving intention for the recipient.

See `purification-rituals.md` for full step-by-step instructions.

---

## 3. Presentation

- Present the Rudraksha in a **beautiful and respectful** manner — a small cloth pouch, wooden
  box, or traditional container is ideal.
- **Explain its significance and benefits** to the recipient so they understand what they are
  receiving and how to care for it.
- Share the basic **do's and don'ts** (see SKILL.md Quick Reference) so they can wear it
  properly from the start.

---

## Key Reminder

Once gifted, the Rudraksha belongs to the recipient alone — it should not be shared with or
worn by others. The recipient should follow all standard wearing and care guidelines as if they
had purchased it themselves.
