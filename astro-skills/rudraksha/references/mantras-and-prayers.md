# Mantras and Prayers for Rudraksha

Chanting specific mantras while wearing Rudraksha beads amplifies their benefits and connects the
wearer with divine energies. All mantras below are chanted **108 times** while holding the bead.

---

## 1. Om Namah Shivaya

**Meaning:** "I bow to Lord Shiva."

**Benefits:** Enhances the spiritual power of Rudraksha beads, promoting peace and protection.

**Usage:** Hold the Rudraksha bead and chant 108 times.

---

## 2. Mahamrityunjaya Mantra

**Mantra:**
> Om Tryambakam Yajamahe Sugandhim Pushtivardhanam |
> Urvarukamiva Bandhanan Mrityor Mukshiya Maamritat ||

**Meaning:** A powerful prayer for healing and protection.

**Benefits:** Invokes Lord Shiva's blessings for longevity, health, and liberation.

**Usage:** Hold the Rudraksha bead and chant 108 times.

---

## 3. Rudra Gayatri Mantra

**Mantra:**
> Om Tatpurushaya Vidmahe Mahadevaya Dhimahi |
> Tanno Rudrah Prachodayat ||

**Meaning:** A prayer to Lord Shiva for wisdom and guidance.

**Benefits:** Enhances spiritual growth and divine connection.

**Usage:** Hold the Rudraksha bead and chant 108 times.

---

## 4. Mukhi-Specific Mantras

Different Rudraksha beads have different numbers of mukhis (faces), each with its own mantra.

| Mukhi | Mantra |
|-------|--------|
| 5-Mukhi | Om Hreem Namah |
| Other mukhis | Consult the specific mantra associated with that bead's ruling deity |

**Tip:** When in doubt, *Om Namah Shivaya* is the universal mantra and works for all Rudraksha beads.

---

## General Guidance

- Chant during or just after purification rituals to energize the bead
- Chant daily as part of your morning routine to maintain the bead's potency
- Chanting during meditation deepens the spiritual connection
