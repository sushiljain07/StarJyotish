# Purification Rituals for Rudraksha

Purifying a Rudraksha bead before wearing it for the first time is essential — it removes
residual energies and infuses the bead with positive vibrations.

---

## 1. Water Purification

**Purpose:** Cleanses impurities and negative energies the bead may have absorbed.

**Steps:**
1. Fill a clean bowl with purified water.
2. Place the Rudraksha bead in the water.
3. Leave it to soak for a few hours or overnight.
4. Remove the bead and pat it dry with a clean cloth.

---

## 2. Milk Purification

**Purpose:** Further purifies the bead and adds a divine touch.

**Steps:**
1. Mix equal parts of milk and water in a clean bowl.
2. Place the Rudraksha bead in the mixture.
3. Leave it to soak for a few hours.
4. Remove the bead and rinse it with clean water.
5. Pat it dry with a clean cloth.

---

## 3. Mantra Purification

**Purpose:** Energizes the bead with sacred vibrations.

**Steps:**
1. Hold the Rudraksha bead in your right hand.
2. Chant *"Om Namah Shivaya"* 108 times.
3. Alternatively, chant other specific mantras associated with that bead's mukhi (face count).
4. Visualize the bead being infused with divine energy.

---

## 4. Panchamrit Purification

**Purpose:** Enhances the bead's spiritual potency.

**Panchamrit mixture:** milk, yogurt, honey, ghee, and sugar.

**Steps:**
1. Mix milk, yogurt, honey, ghee, and sugar in a clean bowl.
2. Place the Rudraksha bead in the mixture.
3. Leave it to soak for a few hours.
4. Remove the bead and rinse it thoroughly with clean water.
5. Pat it dry with a clean cloth.

---

## When to Purify

- Always before wearing for the **first time**
- When **gifting** a Rudraksha to someone — purify before presenting
- Periodically if the bead has been exposed to negative environments
