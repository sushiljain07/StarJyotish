# Wearing & Maintaining Rudraksha

## 1. Wearing Guidelines

| Aspect | Guidance |
|--------|----------|
| **Hand** | Right hand for males; left hand for females |
| **Neck** | Mala worn as a necklace — ensure it rests comfortably on the chest |
| **Skin Contact** | Beads must touch the skin directly to maximize effects |
| **Single Mala** | Wear one mala at a time to avoid mixing energies |
| **Multiple Beads** | If wearing more than one, ensure they are complementary |
| **Position** | Wear the bead close to the heart |

---

## 2. Daily Practices

- **Morning Ritual:** Put on your Rudraksha after a brief prayer or meditation — don't wear it
  before you've centered yourself spiritually.
- **Regular Cleaning:** Clean the beads with water and a soft brush to remove accumulated dirt
  and oils.
- **Daily Mantras:** Chant specific mantras each day to keep the bead energized and effective.

---

## 3. Maintenance Tips

### Physical Care
- **No chemicals:** Keep beads away from harsh soaps, perfumes, and cleaning products — these
  damage the surface.
- **Dry storage:** When not wearing the beads, store them in a dry, clean place. Avoid prolonged
  exposure to moisture.
- **Regular inspection:** Check the bead and string periodically for signs of wear or damage.
  Replace if necessary to maintain effectiveness.

### Spiritual Care
- **Respect:** Treat the bead as a sacred object — keep it clean and off the ground.
- **No sharing:** A Rudraksha absorbs your personal energy; it should only be worn by you.
- **Avoid negative environments:** Do not wear in places with heavy negative energy such as
  funerals or hospitals when possible.

---

## 4. When to Remove

Remove your Rudraksha before:
- Going to sleep (risk of damage and discomfort)
- Consuming alcohol or drugs
- Sexual intercourse
- Visiting a newborn baby
- Entering spaces with heavy negative energy

---

## 5. Re-energizing

If you feel the bead has lost its vitality or has been in a negative environment, re-purify it
using any of the four purification methods (see `purification-rituals.md`) and rechant the
appropriate mantra 108 times.
